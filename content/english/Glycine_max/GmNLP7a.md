## Functional Description:
   - Downregulation of GmNLP7s increased nodule number, and overexpression of **GmNLP7a** (**GmNLP7a**OE) reduced nodule number regardless of nitrate availability, suggesting a negative role for GmNLP7s in nodulation. 
   - Our results show that GmNLP7s play a negative role in soybean nodulation via interaction with GmNINa.
   -  Overexpression of **GmNLP7a** reduced nodule number but not nitrogenase activity
