## Functional Description:
   - Silencing of GmEDS1a/b and GmPAD4 stopped accumulation of salicylic acid and increased the plant's suceptibility to Pseudomonas syringae pv glycinea and soybean mosaic virus infection.
   - GmEDS1a and **GmEDS1b** Interact with AvrA1 and Are Required for the Virulence Activity of This Psg Effector in Soybean
