## Functional Description:
   - The **Lr67** resistance gene encodes a predicted hexose transporter (LR67res) that differs from the susceptible form of the same protein (LR67sus) by two amino acids that are conserved in orthologous hexose transporters. 
   - leaf rust;stripe rust;powdery mildew;stem rust
