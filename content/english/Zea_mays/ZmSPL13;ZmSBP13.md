## Functions:
   - ZmSPL13 and ZmSPL29 as positive regulators of juvenile-to-adult vegetative transition and floral transition in maize.
   - ZmSPL13 and ZmSPL29 positively regulate maize flowering
   - ZmSPL13 and ZmSPL29 directly activate ZmMIR172C expression in leaf to promote juvenile-to-adult vegetative transition
