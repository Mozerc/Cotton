## Functions:
   - ZmCCT9 enhances maize adaptation to higher latitudes.
   - ZmCCT9 is diurnally regulated and negatively regulates the expression of the florigen ZCN8, thereby resulting in late flowering under long days.
   - ZmCCT9 negatively regulates the expression of florigen gene ZCN8, thereby delaying the flowering time of maize under long day conditions.
