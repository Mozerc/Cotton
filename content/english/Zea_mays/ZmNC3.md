## Functions:
   - A HKT1 family gene ZmNC3 (Zea mays L. Na+ Content 3; designated ZmHKT1;2) confers natural variability in shoot-Na+ accumulation and salt tolerance in maize.
   - ZmNC3 confers natural diversity of Na+ content under salt conditions in maize
   - ZmNC3 encodes an Na+-preferring ion transporter
