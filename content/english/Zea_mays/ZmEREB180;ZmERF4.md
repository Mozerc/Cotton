## Functions:
   - ZmEREB180 regulated AR development and reactive oxygen species homeostasis. 
   - ZmEREB180 plays a positive role in maize waterlogging tolerance, seemingly by promoting the formation of adventitious roots and coordinating ROS homeostasis.
   - ZmEREB180 regulates the formation of adventitious roots to enhance waterlogging tolerance.
