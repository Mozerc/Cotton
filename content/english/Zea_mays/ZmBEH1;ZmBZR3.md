## Functions:
   - ZmBEH1 (BZR1/BES1 homolog gene 1) is targeted by ZmLG2 and regulates leaf angle formation by influencing sclerenchyma cell layers on the adaxial side.
   - ZmBEH1 Mu-insertion mutant and CRISPR lines showed reduced leaf angle
   - ZmBEH1 plays a role in leaf angle formation in maize.
