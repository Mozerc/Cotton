## Functional Description:
   - Glossy 8 (**ZmGL8**), which encodes a 3-ketoacyl reductase belonging to the fatty acid elongase complex, in orchestrating wax production and jasmonic acid (JA)–mediated defenses against herbivores in maize (Zea mays).
   - he mutation of GL8 enhanced chemical defenses by activating the JA-dependent pathway.
