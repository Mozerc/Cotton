## Functional Description:
   - INDETERMINATE1 autonomously regulates phosphate homeostasis upstream of the miR399-ZmPHO2 signaling module in maize.
   - **ZmPHO2** was under selection during maize domestication and cultivation, resulting in a more sensitive response to Pi starvation in temperate maize than in tropical maize.
   - Knocking out **ZmPHO2**, a miR399 target, results in premature senescence.
