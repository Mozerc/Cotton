## Functions:
   - MdATG8i interacted with a plastid elongation factor Tu (MdEF-Tu) which became insoluble and aggregated during Vm infection and was degraded through the autophagy pathway.
   - MdATG8i, activated autophagy and contributed to resistance against Valsa canker caused by Valsa Mali (Vm) when being overexpressed in apple.
   - MdATG8i and prevent MdEF-Tu degradation, thus favoring infection.
