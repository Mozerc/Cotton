## Functions:
   - MdERF38 interacted with MdMYB1, a positive modulator of anthocyanin biosynthesis, and facilitated the binding of MdMYB1 to its target genes.  Therefore, MdERF38 promoted anthocyanin biosynthesis in response to drought stress.
