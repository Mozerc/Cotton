## Functional Description:
   - **ZmAVT6** was a amino acid transport-associated gene.
   - **ZmAVT6** can link ABA with nitrogen metabolism, and thus may function in water and nitrogen interactions.
   - **ZmAVT6** was a hub gene in the “purple” module that was induced by water and nitrogen deficiency and is involved in uptake of aspartic acid.
