## Functions:
   - Regulation of a vacuolar proton-pumping P-ATPase MdPH5 by MdMYB73 and its role in malate accumulation and vacuolar acidification.
   - MdPH5 is slightly upregulated in transgenic MdMYB73 callus, which is responsible for malate accumulation and vacuolar acidification.
   - Correlation of MdPH5 expression with malate content during apple fruit ripening and post-harvest.
