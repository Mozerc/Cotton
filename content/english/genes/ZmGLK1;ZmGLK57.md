## Functions:
   - Maize GLKs play an important role in regulating stomatal movements to coordinate photosynthesis and stress tolerance.
   - ZmGLK1 and GOLDEN2 (ZmG2), have shown differential expression patterns between mesophyll cells and the bundle sheath.
   - ZmGLK1 and ZmG2 conferred improved drought tolerance in rice.
