## Functional Description:
   - GmFtsH25 interacted with photosystem I light harvesting complex 2 (**GmLHCa2**), and this interaction may contribute to the observed enhanced photosynthesis.
   - GmFtsH25 interacted with photosystem I light harvesting complex 2 (**GmLHCa2**), and this interaction may contribute to the formation of a favorable chloroplast structure and improve the light energy conversion efficiency.
   - We hypothesize that GmFtsH25 and **GmLHCa2** may form a complex that may directly or indirectly modulate photosynthesis.
