## Functions:
   - The brassinosteroid signaling component SlBZR1 promotes tomato fruit ripening and carotenoid accumulation.
   - Genetic analysis indicated that the BR signaling regulators Brassinazole-resistant1 (SlBZR1) and BRI1-EMS-suppressor1 (SlBES1) act redundantly in fruit softening.
   - Knocking out SlBZR1 inhibited ripening through transcriptome reprogramming at the onset of ripening.
