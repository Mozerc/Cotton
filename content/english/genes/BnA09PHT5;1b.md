## Functions:
   - BnA09PHT5;1b and BnCnPHT5;1b, and uncovered their necessity for cellular Pi homoeostasis through functional analysis.
   - Disruption of BnPHT5;1b genes also caused Pi toxicity in floral organs, reduced seed yield and impacted seed traits, consistent with the proposed role of AtPHT5;1 in floral Pi homoeostasis in Arabidopsis.
   - Highlight the importance of BnPHT5;1b proteins as vacuolar Pi influx transporters in cellular Pi homoeostasis in B. napus.
