## Functions:
   - The expressions of SlACO1 and SlACO3 are significantly increased when tomato fruit ripening is triggered.
   - SlBZR1 directly activates the ethylene biosynthetic genes SlACO1 and SlACO3 to contribute to the ethylene burst and ensure normal fruit ripening.
   - SlBZR1 activates SlACO1 and SlACO3 expression to promote ethylene biosynthesis.
