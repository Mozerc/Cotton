## Functions:
   - TaCYP81D5, one member in a wheat cytochrome P450 gene cluster, confers salinity tolerance via reactive oxygen species scavenging.
   - Constitutively expressing TaCYP81D5 enhanced the salinity tolerance both at seedling and reproductive stages of wheat via accelerating ROS scavenging.
   - TaCYP81D5 confers salinity tolerance in bread wheat and this gene is prospective for crop improvement.
