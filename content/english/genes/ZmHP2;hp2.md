## Functional Description:
   - A naturally occurring non-synonymous SNP variant enhances the interaction between ZmRR1 and **ZmHP2**, conferring maize plants with a salt-hypersensitive phenotype.
   - ZmHP2-mediated signaling increases ZmMATE29 transcription under salt stress conditions. ZmMATE29 encodes a tonoplast-localized Cl− transporter that promotes shoot Cl− exclusion, likely by compartmentalizing Cl− into the vacuoles in root cortex cells.
   - Loss of **ZmHP2** function confers cytokinin-insensitive and salt-hypersensitive phenotype.
