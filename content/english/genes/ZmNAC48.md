## Functions:
   - A natural antisense transcript acts as a negative regulator for the maize drought stress response gene ZmNAC48
   - cis-NATZmNAC48 may negatively regulate ZmNAC48 to affect stomatal closure of maize.
   - The function of the maize ZmNAC48 and its associated cis-NAT proposed in this work will contribute to unravelling the molecular mechanisms mediating drought stress tolerance.
