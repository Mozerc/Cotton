## Functions:
   - The Valsa Mali effector Vm1G-1794 protects the aggregated MdEF-Tu from autophagic degradation to promote infection in apple.
   - MdATG8i interacted with elongation factor MdEF-Tu and altered its subcellular localization.
   - MdEF-Tu negatively regulates apple resistance to V. Mali and was degraded during the infection process.
