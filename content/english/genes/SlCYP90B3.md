## Functions:
   - Exogenous BR treatment and increased endogenous BR contents in tomato plants overexpressing the BR biosynthetic gene SlCYP90B3 promoted ethylene production and fruit ripening.
   - SlCYP90B3 are upregulated at the onset of tomato fruit ripening, followed by an increase in the contents of endogenous brassinolide (BL) and castasterone (CS).
