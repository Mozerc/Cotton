## Functional Description:
   - CRISPR/Cas9-mediated targeted mutagenesis of GmFT2b delays flowering time under LD conditions
   - GmFT2b, promotes flowering under long-days (LDs). Overexpression of GmFT2b upregulates expression of flowering-related genes which are important in regulating flowering time.
