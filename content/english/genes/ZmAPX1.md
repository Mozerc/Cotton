## Functions:
   - This study identified ZmAPX1 as a potentially useful gene for increasing SCLB resistance.
   - ZmAPX1 increases maize resistance against the necrotroph B. maydis by decreasing H2O2 accumulation and activating the jasmonic acid (JA)-mediated defense signaling pathway.
   - ZmAPX1 overexpression enhances maize resistance to SCLB
   - Overexpression of Zea mays ascorbate peroxidase 1 (ZmAPX1) confers resistance against southern corn leaf blight
   - ZmAPX1 modulates the accumulation of H2O2
   - ZmAPX1 affects JA signaling
