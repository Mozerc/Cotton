## Functional Description:
   - The wheat **Sr33** gene functions independently of RAR1, SGT1, and HSP90 chaperones.
   - Lnes carrying the **Sr33** genomic clone show the typical **Sr33**-mediated stem rust resistance.
   - stem rust
