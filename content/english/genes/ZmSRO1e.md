## Functions:
   - ZmSRO1e, in maize as well as in Arabidopsis thaliana increased the sensitivity of the plant to abiotic stress, but repressed anthocyanin biosynthesis and ROS scavenging activity.
   - Mutation of ZmSRO1e enhanced oxidative stress tolerance and anthocyanin accumulation
   - Constitutive expression of ZmSRO1e represses anthocyanin synthesis
