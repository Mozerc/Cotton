## Functional Description:
   - Two candidate genes (**ZmbZIP113** and ZmTSAH1) controlling low-temperature germination ability were identified by QTL-seq and integrative transcriptomic analyses.
   - **ZmbZIP113** was analyzed, revealing a light responsive element (I-box), suggesting that **ZmbZIP113** is involved in the plant response to light.
   - **ZmbZIP113** may positively regulate the RL of IB030. This study was thus able to analyze the LTGA mechanism of MIL-IB030 and identify resistant genes for genetic improvement in maize.
