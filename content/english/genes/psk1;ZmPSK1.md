## Functional Description:
   - ZmIRP, ZmPSK1e, and ZmPIP1 trigger defense gene expression in maize leaves.
   - ZmPSK1e treatment significantly reduced the lesion size in a similar manner as the chitin treatment, suggesting that ZmPSK1e enhances resistance against B. cinerea.
   - ZmPSK1e appeared to cause an increased resistance towards B. cinerea, the phytocytokines Zip1 and ZmIRP promoted infection of the necrotrophic pathogen.
