## Functions:
   - BnaPPT1 is essential for chloroplast development and seed oil accumulation in Brassica napus.
   - BnaPPT1 is required for membrane lipid synthesis and chloroplast development in leaves.
   - BnaPPT1 plays an important role in maintaining plant growth and seed oil accumulation in B. napus.
