## Functions:
   - In which Zcn8 controls 13 tiller-related genes, including four genes for hormonal responses.
   - Zcn8 interacts with Tb1 and then represses D14, which is involved in strigolactone signaling, and two tiller-related genes, Gt1 and Tru1.
   - The promoter element in the Zcn8 promoter repressed Zcn8 transcription and thus decreased tiller number in maize
