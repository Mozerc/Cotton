## Functions:
   - The auxin efflux carrier genes, CsPIN1b (Csa1G025070) and CsPIN3 (Csa5G576590).
   - Elevated expression of CsPIN3 driven by the CsBRC1 promoter results in highly branched cucumber with decreased auxin levels in lateral buds.
   - We found that CsPIN3, but not CsPIN1b, plays an essential role in auxin export during bud outgrowth in cucumber.
