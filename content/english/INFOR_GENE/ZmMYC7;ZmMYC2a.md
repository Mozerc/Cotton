## Functions:
   - ZmMYC7 directly regulates ZmERF147 to increase maize resistance to Fusarium graminearum.
   - ZmMYC7 functions in maize resistance to F. graminearum and sheds light on maize defense responses to pathogenic fungi via the JA signaling pathway.
   - ZmMYC7 is a putative MYC2 ortholog and would share similar functions with OsMYC2 and AtMYC2.
