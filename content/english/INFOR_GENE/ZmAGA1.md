## Functions:
   - ZmAGA1 Hydrolyzes RFOs Late during the Lag Phase of Seed Germination, Shifting Sugar Metabolism toward Seed Germination Over Seed Aging Tolerance
   - Overexpressing ZmAGA1 in Arabidopsis decreased both RFOs and galactinol contents of mature.
   - These data suggest that seed germination and seed aging tolerance can be regulated by manipulation of raffinose/galactinol hydrolysis mediated by ZmAGA1.
   - ZmAGA1 Is Translationally Induced and Capable of Hydrolyzing Galactinol and RFOs during Seed Imbibition
