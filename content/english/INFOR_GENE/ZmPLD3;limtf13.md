## Functions:
   - Loss-of-function alleles of ZmPLD3 cause haploid induction in maize.
   - ZmPLD3 is highly conserved among cereals, highlighting the potential application of these in vivo haploid-inducer lines for other important crop plant species.
   - ZmPLD3 encodes a phospholipase expressed specifically in pollen.
