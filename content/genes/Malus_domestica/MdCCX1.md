## Functional Descriptions:
   - The analysis of Na+ efflux rate in transgenic yeast indicated that **MdCCX1** expression significantly promoted Na+ efflux.
   - **MdCCX1** and MdCCX2 expression levels are significantly and rapidly up-regulated by NaCl treatment.
   - **MdCCX1** significantly promoted Na+ efflux, while MdHB7-like inhibited excessive Na+ accumulation under salt stress by activating **MdCCX1** expression.
