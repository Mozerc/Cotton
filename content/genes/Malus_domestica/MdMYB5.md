## Functional Description:
   - Functional identification of **MdMYB5** involved in secondary cell wallformation in apple.
   - **MdMYB5** is an R2R3 MYB transcription factor localized in the nucleus.
   - **MdMYB5** regulates the formation of secondary cell wall and may correlate with the resistance to salt and osmotic stress in apple.
