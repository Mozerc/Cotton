## Functional Descriptions:
   - An 8-bp deletion in the **MdERF3** promoter disrupted MdDOF5.3 binding, reduced **MdERF3** expression, relieved the inhibition on MdPGLR3, MdPME2, and MdACO4 expression, and ultimately decreased flesh firmness and crispness retainability. 
   - Role of **MdERF3** and MdERF118 natural variations in apple flesh firmness/crispness retainability and development of QTL-based genomics-assisted prediction.
   - **MdERF3** was then transiently overexpressed via Agrobacterium-mediated infiltration or silenced via pTRV virus-induced gene silencing (VIGS).
   - These data further confirmed that **MdERF3** contributed to post-harvest flesh firmness/crispness retainability.
