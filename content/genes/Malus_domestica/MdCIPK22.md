## Functional Descriptions:
   - **MdCIPK22** increased ABA sensitivity in an MdAREB2-dependent manner.
   - **MdCIPK22** was required for ABA-induced phosphorylation at Thr411 of the MdAREB2 protein and enhanced its stability and transcriptional activity.
   - **MdCIPK22** improves ABA sensitivity in an MdAREB2-dependent manner.
   - An apple sucrose transporter MdSUT2.2 is a phosphorylation target for protein kinase **MdCIPK22** in response to drought.
   - A series of transgenic analysis showed that **MdCIPK22** was required for the drought-induced phosphylation at Ser(381) site of MdSUT2.2 protein, and that it enhanced the stability and transport activity of MdSUT2.2 protein.
