## Functional Description:
   - **MdMYB8** is associated with flavonol biosynthesis via the activation of the MdFLS promoter in the fruits of Malus crabapple.
   - Stable overexpression or RNAi knockdown of **MdMYB8** in transgenic ‘Orin’ apple calli resulted in a higher or lower flavonol content.
   - **MdMYB8** is a regulator of flavonol biosynthesis.
