## Functional Descriptions:
   - **SAUR32** to modulate the Fe-deficiency response, following the upregulation of proton extrusion from the root.
   - A long non-coding apple RNA, MSTRG.85814.11, acts as a transcriptional enhancer of **SAUR32** and contributes to the Fe-deficiency response.
   - MSTRG.85814.11 acts through the lncRNA-**SAUR32** module to modulate the Fe-deficiency response.
