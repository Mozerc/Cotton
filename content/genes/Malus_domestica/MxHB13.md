## Functional Descriptions:
   - An HD-ZIP transcription factor, **MxHB13**, integrates auxin-regulated and juvenility-determined control of adventitious rooting in Malus xiaojinensis.
   - An HD-ZIP transcription factor gene, **MxHB13**, was found to be under negative regulation of MdSPL26 by directly binding to **MxHB13** promoter.
   - A model is proposed in which **MxHB13** links juvenility-limited and auxin-limited AR recalcitrance mechanisms in Mx-A.
   - Among the differentially expressed genes, an HD-ZIP transcription factor gene, **MxHB13**, was found to be under negative regulation of MdSPL26 by directly binding to **MxHB13** promoter. **MxHB13** binds to the promoter of MxABCB19-2 and positively affects the expression.
