## Functional Descriptions:
   - The **MdBBX22**–miR858–MdMYB9/11/12 module regulates proanthocyanidin biosynthesis in apple peel.
   - Under light stress, **MdBBX22** induced mdm-miR858 expression to inhibit PA accumulation and thereby indirectly enhanced anthocyanin synthesis in the peel.
   - **MdBBX22** inhibits MdMYB9/11/12-induced PA accumulation by promoting the expression of mdm-miR858 in apple calli.
   - Overexpression of **MdBBX22** induced the expression of mdm-miR858 to inhibit the accumulation of PAs in apple calli overexpressing MdMYB9/11/12.
