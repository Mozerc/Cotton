## Functional Descriptions:
   - Variation in the promoter of the sorbitol dehydrogenase gene **MdSDH2** affects binding of the transcription factor MdABI3 and alters fructose content in apple fruit.
   - A putative Fru content control gene – **MdSDH2** – was found.
   - Our results not only demonstrate the major role of **MdSDH2** in altering Fru content, but also offer evidence that ABA signaling is involved in Fru accumulation in apple fruit.
   - **MdSDH2** can alter Fru content as the major regulatory gene and that ABA signaling might be involved in Fru content accumulation in apple fruit.
