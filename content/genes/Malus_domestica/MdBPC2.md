## Functional Descriptions:
   - The transcription factor **MdBPC2** alters apple growth and promotes dwarfing by regulating auxin biosynthesis.
   - Overexpression of **MdBPC2** in apple decreased plant height, altered leaf morphology, and inhibited root system development.
   - **MdBPC2** altered auxin content.
   - **MdBPC2** inhibits transcription of MdYUC2a and MdYUC6b.
