## Functional Descriptions:
   - **MxMPK4-1** phosphorylated the C terminus of MxRBOHD2 at Ser797 and Ser906 and positively and negatively regulated NOX activity through these phospho-sites, respectively.  When compared with apple calli that overexpressed MxRBOHD2, the coexpression of **MxMPK4-1** and MxRBOHD2 prominently enhanced the Fe deficiency responses.
   - Overexpression of **MxMPK4-1** in apple calli and apple roots enhanced the responses to Fe deficiency.
   - **MxMPK4-1** interacted with NADPH oxidases (NOX)—respiratory burst oxidase homologs MxRBOHD1 and MxRBOHD2, which positively regulated responses to Fe deficiency. 
   - The coexpression of **MxMPK4-1** and MxRBOHD2 prominently enhanced the Fe deficiency responses.
