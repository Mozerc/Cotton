## Functional Descriptions:
   - We observed that **MdACS3a**-overexpression promoted ethylene production, resulting in fruit softening, while ethylene production decreased and firmness was higher in **MdACS3a**-silenced fruit compared to the control.
   - Previous studies have shown that **MdACS3a** is important for ethylene biosynthesis, and hence the timing of the ethylene peak.
   - We demonstrate that an early-maturing-specific acetylation H3K9ac peak in **MdACS3a** and expression of **MdACS3a** were specifically up-regulated in fruit of an early-maturing, but not a late-maturing, cultivar.
