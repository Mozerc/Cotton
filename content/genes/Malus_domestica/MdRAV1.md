## Functional Description:
   - The MdWRKY31 transcription factor binds to the **MdRAV1** promoter to mediate ABA sensitivity.
   - We also found that **MdRAV1** binds directly to the promoters of MdABI3 and MdABI4 and repressed their expression.
   - **MdRAV1** directly binds to promoters of MdABIs to repress their expression.
