## Functional Descriptions:
   - Here, we revealed that MdBT2 inhibits adventitious root (AR) formation through interacting with AUXIN RESPONSE FACTOR8 (MdARF8) and INDOLE-3-ACETIC ACID INDUCIBLE3 (**MdIAA3**).
   - **MdIAA3** inhibited AR formation by forming heterodimers with MdARF8 as well as other MdARFs (MdARF5, MdARF6, MdARF7, and MdARF19).
   - MdBT2 facilitated **MdIAA3** stability and slightly promoted its interaction with MdARF8.
