## Functional Descriptions:
   - The upregulation of transcription factor **MdMYB1r1** directly activated MdBGLU40 and the binding affinity of **MdMYB1r1** to the MdBGLU40 promoter increased in HK apple tissue, leading to high levels of coumarin and resistance in HK apple.
   - We found coumarin synthesis was regulated by the transcription factor **MdMYB1r1** whose affinity to MdBGLU40 involved in coumarin synthesis was increased with K concentration. 
   - **MdMYB1r1** was responsible for initiating coumarin synthesis to confer apple resistance under HK conditions.
   - Overexpression and suppression of MdBGLU40 and **MdMYB1r1** in apple calli.
