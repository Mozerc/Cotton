## Functional Description:
   - APPLE VACUOLAR PROCESSING ENZYME 4 (**MdVPE4**) is involved in resistance to B. dothidea.
   - **MdVPE4** silencing reduced fruit disease resistance, whereas its overexpression improved resistance.
   - The interaction between **MdVPE4** and MdCPI1 plays an important role in modulating fruit disease resistance to B. dothidea.
