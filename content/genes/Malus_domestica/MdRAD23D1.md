## Functional Descriptions:
   - The ubiquitin-binding protein **MdRAD23D1** mediates drought response by regulating degradation of the proline-rich protein MdPRP6 in apple.
   - A shuttle protein, **MdRAD23D1**, mediated drought response in apple plants
   - **MdRAD23D1**-MdPRP6” conferred drought stress tolerance in apple plants.
   - **MdRAD23D1** levels increased under drought stress, and its suppression resulted in decreased stress tolerance in apple plants.
