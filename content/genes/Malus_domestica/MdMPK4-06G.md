## Functional Description:
   - MdMPK4 proteins interact with MdMYB1 and are phosphorylated in response to light.
   - MdMPK4 proteins are involved in anthocyanin accumulation in apple peels.
   - MdMPK4 proteins promote anthocyanin biosynthesis
