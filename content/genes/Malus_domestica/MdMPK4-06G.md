## Functional Descriptions:
   - **MdMPK4** proteins interact with MdMYB1 and are phosphorylated in response to light.
   - **MdMPK4** proteins are involved in anthocyanin accumulation in apple peels.
   - Transient **MdMPK4** and MdMYB1 overexpression assays further revealed that light-induced anthocyanin accumulation relies on MdMPK4 kinase activity, which is required for maximum MdMYB1 activity.
   - Apple **MPK4** mediates phosphorylation of MYB1 to enhance light-induced anthocyanin accumulation.
   - Phosphorylation of MdERF17 by **MdMPK4** promotes apple fruit peel degreening during light/dark transitions.
