## Functional Descriptions:
   - **MdPHR1** interacted with MdWRKY75, a positive regulator of anthocyanin biosynthesis, to enhance the MdWRKY75-activated transcription of MdMYB1, leading to anthocyanin accumulation. In addition, the E3 ubiquitin ligase SEVEN IN ABSENTIA1 (MdSINA1) negatively regulated **MdPHR1**-promoted anthocyanin biosynthesis via the ubiquitination-mediated degradation of **MdPHR1**. 
   - **MdPHR1** was essential for Pi deficiency-induced anthocyanin accumulation in apple.
   - Taken together, these findings not only demonstrate the regulatory role of **MdPHR1** in Pi starvation induced anthocyanin accumulation, but also provide an insight into the post-translational regulation of PHR1.
   - **MdPHR1** positively regulates Pi deficiency-induced anthocyanin accumulation.
   - **MdPHR1** regulates anthocyanin accumulation in an MdWRKY75-dependent manner.
