## Functional Descriptions:
   - Further studies showed that MdBBX37 interacted with the senescence regulatory protein **MdbHLH93** to enhance its transcriptional activation on the senescence-associated gene MdSAG18, thereby promoting leaf senescence.
   - As an interaction partner of **MdbHLH93**, we hypothesize that MdBBX37 may be involved in the modulation of leaf senescence.
   - The modulation of leaf senescence by MdBBX37 partly depends on **MdbHLH93**.
   - MdBBX37 interacted with the senescence regulatory protein **MdbHLH93** to enhance its transcriptional activation on the senescence-associated gene MdSAG18, thereby promoting leaf senescence.
   - **MdbHLH93**, an apple activator regulating leaf senescence, is regulated by ABA and MdBT2 in antagonistic ways.
