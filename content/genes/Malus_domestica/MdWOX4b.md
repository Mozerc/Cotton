## Functional Descriptions:
   - Genome-wide identification of WOX gene family in apple and a functional analysis of **MdWOX4b** during adventitious root formation.
   - The MdWOX genes exhibit tissue-specific expression patterns and MdWOX4a, **MdWOX4b**, MdWOX5b, MdWOX11/12a, and MdWOX11/12b may play essential roles in adventitious root development.
   - Heterologous overexpression of **MdWOX4b** in tobacco enhanced adventitious rooting ability.
   - The adventitious rooting ability was enhanced in **MdWOX4b** transgenic tobacco lines.
