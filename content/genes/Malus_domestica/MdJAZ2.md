## Functional Descriptions:
   - **MdJAZ2** interacts with MdBBX37 and alleviates MdBBX37-accelerated leaf senescence.
   - The **MdJAZ2**-MdBBX37-MdbHLH93-MdSAG18 and MdSINA3-MdBBX37 modules realized the precise modulation of JA on leaf senescence.
   - The JA signaling repressor **MdJAZ2** interacted with MdBBX37 and interfered with the interaction between MdBBX37 and MdbHLH93, thereby negatively mediating MdBBX37-promoted leaf senescence.
