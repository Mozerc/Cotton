## Functional Description:
   - The regulatory module **MdPUB29**-MdbHLH3 connects ethylene biosynthesis with fruit quality in apple.
   - High glucose-inhibited U-box-type E3 ubiquitin ligase **MdPUB29**, the ortholog of Arabidopsis AtPUB29 in apple.
   - Ubiquitin E3 ligase **MdPUB29** interacts with the MdbHLH3 protein to promote its degradation.
