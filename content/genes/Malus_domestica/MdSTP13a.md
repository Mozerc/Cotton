## Functional Descriptions:
   - **MdSTP13a**-mediated direct uptake of Suc is primarily responsible for apple pollen tube growth on Suc medium.
   - Antisense Repression of **MdSTP13a** Decreases Pollen Tube Growth on Glc.
   - **MdSTP13a** Localizes to Pollen Plasma Membrane.
   - These findings demonstrate that **MdSTP13a** takes up both hexose and Suc for sorbitol-modulated pollen tube growth in apple, revealing a situation where acquisition of sugars for pollen tube growth is regulated by a sugar alcohol.
