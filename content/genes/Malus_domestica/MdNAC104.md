## Functional Descriptions:
   - **MdNAC104** positively regulates apple cold tolerance via CBF-dependent and CBF-independent pathways.
   - Under cold stress, **MdNAC104**-overexpressing transgenic plants exhibited less ion leakage and lower ROS (reactive oxygen species) accumulation, but higher contents of osmoregulatory substances and activities of antioxidant enzymes.
   - In conclusion, this study revealed the **MdNAC104** regulatory mechanism of cold tolerance in apple via CBF-dependent and CBF-independent pathways.
