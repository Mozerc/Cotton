## Functional Descriptions:
   - **MdMYB30** directly binds to the β-KETOACYL-COA SYNTHASE 1 (MdKCS1) promoter to activate its expression and promote wax biosynthesis. 
   - Apple SUMO E3 ligase MdSIZ1 regulates cuticular wax biosynthesis by SUMOylating transcription factor **MdMYB30**.
   - These findings indicate that the MdSIZ1–**MdMYB30**–MdKCS1 module positively regulates cuticular wax biosynthesis in apples.
   - **MdMYB30** functions as a target protein of MdSIZ1 through SUMOylation in the regulation of apple cuticular wax accumulation.
