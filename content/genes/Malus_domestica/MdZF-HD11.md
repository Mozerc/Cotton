## Functional Descriptions:
   - The apple transcription factor **MdZF-HD11** regulates fruit softening by promoting Mdβ-GAL18 expression.
   - **MdZF-HD11** interacts with Mdβ-GAL18 to promote the post-harvest softening of apple.
   - Both **MdZF-HD11** and Mdβ-GAL18 genes were up-regulated by exogenous ethylene treatment and repressed by 1-methylcyclopropene treatment.
   - The overexpression of **MdZF-HD11** in tomato also promoted fruit softening.
