## Functional Descriptions:
   - MdMYB30 directly binds to the β-KETOACYL-COA SYNTHASE 1 (**MdKCS1**) promoter to activate its expression and promote wax biosynthesis. 
   - These findings indicate that the MdSIZ1–MdMYB30–**MdKCS1** module positively regulates cuticular wax biosynthesis in apples.
   - MdSIZ1 mediates the SUMOylation and stabilization of MdMYB30, which directly activates the transcription of **MdKCS1** to promote cuticular wax accumulation in the apple.
   - The MdSIZ1–MdMYB30–**MdKCS1** module might be a key regulator in apple cuticular wax biosynthesis.
