## Functional Descriptions:
   - miRNA_157-CsCuRX, miRNA_411-CsGH3.6, and miRNA_161/297/257-**CsHD-Zip** might be responsible for corolla opening in the super ovary in cucumber.
   - The down-regulated **CsHD-Zip** gene (Csa6G525430) identified in this study was regulated by 28 up-regulated miRNAs at the yellow bud stage. 
   - miRNA_161, miRNA_297, and miRNA_257 were highly expressed at the yellow bud stage, suggesting that they regulate the **CsHD-Zip** response to corolla opening in the cucumber super ovary.
