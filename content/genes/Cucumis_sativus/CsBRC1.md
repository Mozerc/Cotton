## Functional Description:
   - **CsBRC1** inhibits axillary bud outgrowth by directly repressing the auxin efflux carrier CsPIN3 in cucumber.
   - cucumber BRANCHED1 (**CsBRC1**) is found to inhibit shoot branching by directly repressing the auxin efflux carrier CsPIN3.
   - A regulatory pathway of **CsBRC1**–CsPIN3–auxin transport in suppressing shoot branching in cucumber.
