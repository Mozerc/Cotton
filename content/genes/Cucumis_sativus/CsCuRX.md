## Functional Descriptions:
   - miRNA_157-**CsCuRX**, miRNA_411-CsGH3.6, and miRNA_161/297/257-CsHD-Zip might be responsible for corolla opening in the super ovary in cucumber.
   - **CsCuRX**, which was identified during corolla development of the super and normal ovaries, and encodes a Cupredoxin protein.
   - Those results and our data strongly suggest that the miRNA_157 target **CsCuRX** regulates corolla development in the super ovaries in cucumber.
