## Functional Descriptions:
   - Overexpression of **DgATX** led to increased tolerance, whereas the **DgATX** mutant resulted in decreased tolerance of chrysanthemum to cold stresses.
   - **DgATX** expression was also responsive to low temperature.
   - The dgmyb **DgATX** double mutant was more susceptible to low temperature than the dgmyb and **DgATX** mutants.
