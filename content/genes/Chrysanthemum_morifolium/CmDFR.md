## Functional Descriptions:
   - **CmDFR**, which encodes the key enzyme in anthocyanin biosynthesis.
   - In Chrysanthemum morifolium, MYB6-bHLH2 cooperates to promote the expression of anthocyanin-associated structural genes, including **CmDFR**, to affect the accumulation of anthocyanins.
   - These results suggest that CmNAC25 positively regulates anthocyanin biosynthesis by activating the expression of CmMYB6 and **CmDFR**.
