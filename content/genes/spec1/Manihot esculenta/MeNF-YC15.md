## Functional Descriptions:
   - NF-YC15 transcription factor activates ethylene biosynthesis and improves cassava disease resistance.
   - Identified **MeNF-YC15** gene in the NF-Y family that significantly responded to Xanthomonas axonopodis pv. manihotis (Xam) treatment.
   - Using **MeNF-YC15**-silenced and -overexpressed cassava plants, we elucidated that **MeNF-YC15** positively regulated disease resistance to cassava bacterial blight (CBB).
   - The physical interaction between 2C-type protein phosphatase 1 (MePP2C1) and **MeNF-YC15** inhibited the transcriptional activation of MeACO1 by **MeNF-YC15**.
   - MePP2C1-**MeNF-YC15** interaction modulates ethylene biosynthesis and cassava disease resistance, providing gene network for cassava genetic improvement.
