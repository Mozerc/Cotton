## Functional Descriptions:
   - 12 genes in eight types, including ZmbHLH51, ZmbHLH122, ZmTGA9-1/-2/-3, **ZmTGA10|Zmbzip7**, ZmMYB84, ZmMYB33-1/-2, ZmPHD11 and ZmLBD10/27, were identified as maize new GMS genes by using DNA sequencing, phenotypic and cytological analyses.
   - The knock-out mutants of **ZmTGA10|Zmbzip7** showed nearly normal pollen development, but the mutant anthers failed to dehisce.
   - These findings proved that **ZmTGA10|Zmbzip7** functions in maize male fertility mainly by controlling anther dehiscence.
   - Besides the five maize GMS TFs in the core regulatory pathway, three-type GMS TF genes identified here (**ZmTGA10|Zmbzip7**, ZmPHD11 and ZmMYB33-1/2) are the maize orthologs of AtTGA10/OsTGA10, AtMMD1/OsTIP3 and AtMYB33/65/OsGAMYB in Arabidopsis and rice, respectively, and may play the similar and conserved roles in controlling male sterility in plants.
