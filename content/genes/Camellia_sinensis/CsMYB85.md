## Functional Descriptions:
   - The MYB TFs **CsMYB85** involved in regulating biosynthesis of caffeine.
   - **CsMYB85**, a putative PA biosynthesis activator.
   - Three representative MYBs, CsMYB66 from the AtMYB5 subclade, **CsMYB85** from the VvMYBPA1 subclade, and CsMYB196 from the AtTT2 subclade, were further verified for their functions in regulating PA biosynthesis in planta.
