## Functional Descriptions:
   - Overexpression of soybean **GmDREB1** enhances drought stress tolerance of transgenic wheat in the field.
   - Overexpressed **GmDREB1** from soybean in two popular wheat varieties and conducted drought-tolerance experiments across a range of years, sites, and drought-stress regimes.
   - Overexpression of the soybean **GmDREB1** transcription factor in wheat improves growth under drought stress.
   - Our results demonstrate the utility of transgenic overexpression of **GmDREB1** to improve the drought tolerance of wheat in the field.
