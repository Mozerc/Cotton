## Functional Descriptions:
   - The apple C2H2-type zinc finger transcription factor **MdZAT10** positively regulates JA-induced leaf senescence by interacting with MdBT2.
   - **MdZAT10**, a C2H2-type zinc finger transcription factor (TF) in apple, markedly accelerates leaf senescence and increases the expression of senescence-related genes.
   - **MdZAT10** was found to enhance the transcriptional activity of MdABI5 for MdNYC1 and MdNYE1, thus accelerating leaf senescence.
   - We also found that the JA-responsive protein MdBT2 directly interacts with **MdZAT10** and reduces its protein stability through ubiquitination and degradation, thereby delaying **MdZAT10**-mediated leaf senescence.
