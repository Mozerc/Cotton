## Functional Descriptions:
   - MdMYB10 affects nitrogen uptake and reallocation by regulating the nitrate transporter **MdNRT2.4-1** in red-fleshed apple.
   - Expression analysis revealed that nitrate starvation induced **MdNRT2.4-1** expression and high nitrate reduced **MdNRT2.4-1** expression. Elevated vacuolar membrane H+-ATPase mediated by MdMYB10 can also supply energy for ion storage, increasing the nitrate content in the vacuole.
   - Under high nitrate conditions, GUS activity was repressed, demonstrating that the expression of **MdNRT2.4-1** was negatively regulated under high nitrate conditions.
   - The extent to which **MdNRT2.4-1** regulates MdMYB10-enhanced nitrate uptake and usage remains to be clarified in future research.
