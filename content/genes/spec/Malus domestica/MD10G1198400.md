## Functional Descriptions:
   - miR164g-**MsNAC022** acts as a novel module mediating drought response by transcriptional regulation of reactive oxygen species scavenging systems in apple.
   - Overexpression of **MsNAC022** in Arabidopsis and the cultivated apple line ‘GL-3’ (Malus domestica Borkh.) lead to enhanced tolerance to drought stress by raising the ROS scavenging enzymes activity and related genes expression levels, particularly PEROXIDASE (MsPOD).
   - Our findings reveal the function of the miR164g-**MsNAC022** module in mediating the drought response of M. sieversii and lay a foundation for breeding drought-tolerant apple cultivars.
   - These results suggest that plant resistance to drought, osmotic and salt stresses are negatively and positively modulated by msi-miR164g and **MsNAC022**, respectively.
