## Functional Descriptions:
   - **ZmABA2|sca1**, an interacting protein of ZmMPK5, is involved in abscisic acid biosynthesis and functions.
   - Phosphorylation of Ser173 in **ZmABA2|sca1** by ZmMPK5 was shown to increase the activity of **ZmABA2|sca1** and the protein stability.
   - Various abiotic stimuli induced the expression of **ZmABA2|sca1** in leaves of maize plants. Pharmacological, biochemical and molecular biology and genetic analyses showed that both ZmMPK5 and **ZmABA2|sca1** coordinately regulate the content of ABA.
   - Overexpression of **ZmABA2|sca1** in tobacco plants was found to elevate the content of ABA, regulate seed germination and root growth under drought and salt stress and enhance the tolerance of tobacco plants to drought and salt stress.
   - **ZmABA2|sca1** is a direct target of ZmMPK5 and is involved in ABA biosynthesis and functions.
