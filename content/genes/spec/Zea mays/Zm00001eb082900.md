## Functional Descriptions:
   - **ZmZHOUPI|ZmZOU|o11**, an endosperm-specific basic helix–loop–helix transcription factor involved in maize seed development.
   - In maize (Zea mays), a cereal with a persistent endosperm, a single gene, **ZmZHOUPI|ZmZOU|o11**, falls into the same phylogenetic clade as AtZOU. Its expression is limited to the endosperm where it peaks during the filling stage.
   - Ectopic expression of **ZmZHOUPI|ZmZOU|o11** reduces stomatal number, possibly due to inappropriate protein interactions.
   - **ZmZHOUPI|ZmZOU|o11** is an endosperm-specific bHLH transcription factor, **ZmZHOUPI|ZmZOU|o11** knock-down did not significantly modify endosperm growth but slightly impeded embryo growth at early stages.
