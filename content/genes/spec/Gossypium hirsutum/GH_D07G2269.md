## Functional Descriptions:
   - **GhMYB30**-GhMUR3 affects fiber elongation and secondary wall thickening in cotton.
   - Overall, these results revealed that GhMUR3 under the regulation of **GhMYB30** and plays an essential role in cotton fiber elongation and secondary wall thickening.
   - **GhMYB30** plays an important role in the regulation of fiber development and regulates fiber secondary wall synthesis by inhibiting the expression of GhMYB46.
