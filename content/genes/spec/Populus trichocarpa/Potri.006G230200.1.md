## Functional Descriptions:
   - Poplar calcineurin B-like proteins PtCBL10A and **PtCBL10B** regulate shoot salt tolerance through interaction with PtSOS2 in the vacuolar membrane.
   - Both PtCBL10A and **PtCBL10B** were targeted to the tonoplast and expression of either one in the Arabidopsis cbl10 mutant could rescue its shoot salt-sensitive phenotype.
   - Overexpression of either PtCBL10A or **PtCBL10B** conferred salt tolerance on transgenic poplar plants by maintaining ion homeostasis in shoot tissues under salinity stress. 
   - PtCBL10s and PtSOS3 overexpression differentially regulates tissue Na+ and K+ distribution.
   - PtCBL10A and **PtCBL10B** encode two putative calcium sensors in Populus.
