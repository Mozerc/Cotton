## Functional Descriptions:
   - The SmNPR4-**SmTGA5** module regulates SA-mediated phenolic acid biosynthesis in Salvia miltiorrhiza hairy roots.
   - A basic leucine zipper family transcription factor **SmTGA5** was identified and was found to interact with SmNPR4. **SmTGA5** activates the expression of phenolic acid biosynthesis gene SmTAT1 through binding to the as-1 element.
   - **SmTGA5** is a positive regulator of salvianolic acid biosynthesis, where SmTAT1 is a target gene of **SmTGA5**.
   - These findings suggest that SmNPR4 inhibits the function of **SmTGA5** in regulation of phenolic acid biosynthesis.
