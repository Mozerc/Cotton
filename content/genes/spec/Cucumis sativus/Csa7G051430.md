## Functional Descriptions:
   - By re-sequencing of bulked segregants, we identified the candidate gene Csa7G051430 encoding ACCUMULATION AND REPLICATION OF CHLOROPLASTS 5 (ARC5) that plays a vital role in chloroplast division in Arabidopsis.
   - Using whole genome re-sequencing and genetic mapping, we demonstrated a non-synonymous mutation in the cucumber ARC5 homolog (**CsaARC5**) underlying the mutant phenotype. In agreement with the role of ARC5 in chloroplast division, we observed abnormal chloroplast development in fruit exocarp protoplasts in the mutant line.
   - The results indicated that the reduced expression of **CsaARC5** might block chloroplast division in cucumber to some extent, and supported that **CsaARC5** as the gene underlying the lgp mutant.
