## Functional Descriptions:
   - Transcriptomic changes triggered by carotenoid biosynthesis inhibitors and role of Citrus sinensis phosphate transporter 4|**CsPHT4|2** (**CsPHT4|2**|**CsPHT4|2**) in enhancing carotenoid accumulation.
   - Using weighted correlation network analysis, a plastid-type phosphate transporter 4|**CsPHT4|2** (**CsPHT4|2**|**CsPHT4|2**) was identified as closely correlated with high-lycopene accumulation induced by CPTA.
   - Over-expression of **CsPHT4|2**|**CsPHT4|2** enhanced carotenoid accumulation over tenfold in ‘Cara Cara’ sweet orange callus-derived juice vesicles.
   - Transient over-expression of **CsPHT4|2**|**CsPHT4|2** significantly enhanced carotenoid accumulation over tenfold in ‘Cara Cara’ sweet orange juice vesicle-derived callus.
