## Functional Descriptions:
   - Barley **HvMyo11A1|Ror1** encodes a class XI myosin required for mlo-based broad-spectrum resistance to the fungal powdery mildew pathogen.
   - Single amino acid substitutions of this myosin, deduced from non-functional **HvMyo11A1|Ror1** mutant alleles, map to the nucleotide-binding region and the interface between the relay-helix and the converter domain of the motor protein.
   - **HvMyo11A1|Ror1** myosin accumulates transiently in the course of powdery mildew infection. Functional fluorophore-labeled **HvMyo11A1|Ror1** variants associate with mobile intracellular compartments that partially colocalize with peroxisomes. 
   - Single-cell expression of the **HvMyo11A1|Ror1** tail region causes a dominant-negative effect that phenocopies **HvMyo11A1|Ror1** loss-of-function mutants.
