## Functional Descriptions:
   - Eggplant transcription factor **SmMYB5** integrates jasmonate and light signaling during anthocyanin biosynthesis.
   - A JA response factor, **SmMYB5**, as an anthocyanin positive regulator by applying RNA-sequencing and characterization of transgenic plants.
   - Delineated a light-dependent JA-**SmMYB5** signaling pathway that promotes anthocyanin synthesis in eggplant fruit skins.
