## Functional Descriptions:
   - A light-responsive transcription factor **SmMYB35** enhances anthocyanin biosynthesis in eggplant.
   - **SmMYB35**, a light-responsive R2R3-MYB transcription factor, positively regulates anthocyanin biosynthesis in eggplant by binding to the promoters of SmCHS, SmF3H, SmDFR, and SmANS and enhancing their activities.
   - **SmMYB35** interacts with SmTT8 and SmTTG1 to form a MBW complex, thereby enhancing anthocyanin.
   - The results suggested that the expression of **SmMYB35** was regulated by SmHY5 through directly binding to G-box in the promoter region, and the overexpression of **SmMYB35** could increase the anthocyanin content in the stems and petals of the transgenic eggplants.
