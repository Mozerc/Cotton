## Functional Descriptions:
   - **BcNAC056**-GFP was located in the nucleus at the subcellular level, and **BcNAC056** was responsive to leaf senescence and different hormones at the transcriptional level.
   - Heterologous overexpression of **BcNAC056** in Arabidopsis promoted leaf senescence, accompanied by the increased expression of senescence-associated genes (SAGs), whereas virus-induced gene silencing–based silencing in pak choi delayed leaf senescence.
   - **BcNAC056**–BcWRKY1 interaction promoted the activation of BcSAG12. Taken together, our findings revealed a new regulatory mechanism of leaf senescence in pak choi.
