## Functional Descriptions:
   - **CmCNIH1** improves salt tolerance by influencing the trafficking of CmHKT1|1 in pumpkin.
   - **CmCNIH1** was a key component of the cellular vesicle transport machinery located in the endoplasmic reticulum (ER), ER export site and Golgi apparatus. A **CmCNIH1** knockout mutant was more sensitive to salt stress than the wild-type (WT).
   - **CmCNIH1** may function as a cargo receptor that regulates the localization of CmHKT1|1 to the PM to improve salt tolerance in pumpkin.
   - **CmCNIH1** did not directly affect the ion permeability of CmHKT1|1, but it participated in salt stress response by regulating the membrane localization of CmHKT1|1.
   - **CmCNIH1**, and its mutants displayed reduced salt tolerance of pumpkin seedlings. **CmCNIH1** did not directly affect the ion permeability of CmHKT1|1, but it participated in salt stress response by regulating the membrane localization of CmHKT1|1.
