## Functional Descriptions:
   - Auxin inhibits lignin and cellulose biosynthesis in stone cells of pear fruit via the PbrARF13-**PbrNSC**-PbrMYB132 transcriptional regulatory cascade.
   - **PbrNSC** was shown to bind to an auxin response factor, PbrARF13,**PbrNSC** was identified as a positive regulator of PbrMYB132 through analyses of co-expression network of stone cell formation-related genes.
   - The ‘PbrARF13-**PbrNSC**-PbrMYB132’ regulatory cascade mediates the biosynthesis of lignin and cellulose in stone cells of pear fruit in response to auxin signals and also provides new insights into plant SCW formation.
   - Our research identified an important positive regulator of lignin and cellulose biosynthesis in stone cells, **PbrNSC**, through eQTL and co-expression network analyses of lignin and cellulose pathway-related genes.
