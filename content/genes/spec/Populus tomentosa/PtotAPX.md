## Functional Descriptions:
   - Chloroplast thylakoid ascorbate peroxidase **PtotAPX** plays a key role in chloroplast development by decreasing hydrogen peroxide in Populus tomentosa.
   - Suppression of **PtotAPX** expression retards chloroplast development, while overexpression of **PtotAPX** promotes chloroplast development.
   - **PtotAPX**-mediated removal of ROS is critical for the development of chloroplasts from proplastids upon exposure to light.
   - Based on these results, we propose that **PtotAPX**-mediated removal of reactive oxygen species plays a key role in chloroplast development from proplastids upon exposure to light in P. tomentosa.
