## Functional Descriptions:
   - Overexpression of **MsDREB6.2** results in cytokinin-deficient developmental phenotypes and enhances drought tolerance in transgenic apple plants.
   - Overexpression of **MsDREB6.2** increased the expression of a key cytokinin (CK) catabolism gene, MdCKX4a, which led to a significant reduction in endogenous CK levels, and caused a decrease in shoot:root ratio in transgenic apple plants. 
   - Overexpression of **MsDREB6.2** resulted in a decrease in stomatal aperture and density and an increase in root hydraulic conductance (L0), and thereby enhanced drought tolerance in transgenic plants.
   - **MsDREB6.2** enhances drought tolerance and that its function may be due, at least in part, to its influence on stomatal opening, root growth, and AQP expression.
