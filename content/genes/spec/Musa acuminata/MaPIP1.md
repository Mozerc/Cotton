## Functional Descriptions:
   - A banana aquaporin gene, **MaPIP1|1**|**MaPIP1|1**, is involved in tolerance to drought and salt stresses.
   - These results indicate that **MaPIP1|1**|**MaPIP1|1** is targeted to the plasma membrane.
   - Reduced expression of ABA-responsive genes in **MaPIP1|1**|**MaPIP1|1**-overexpressing plants reflects their improved physiological status.
   - Our results demonstrated that heterologous expression of banana **MaPIP1|1**|**MaPIP1|1** in Arabidopsis confers salt and drought stress tolerances by reducing membrane injury, improving ion distribution and maintaining osmotic balance.
