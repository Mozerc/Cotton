## Functional Descriptions:
   - The banana fruit Dof transcription factor **MaDof23** acts as a repressor and interacts with MaERF9 in regulating ripening-related genes.
   - **MaDof23** is a transcriptional repressor, whereas MaERF9 is a transcriptional activator.
   - These in vivo data demonstrate that **MaDof23** may act as a transcriptional repressor, and MaERF9 as a transcriptional activator.
