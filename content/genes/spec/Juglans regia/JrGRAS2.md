## Functional Descriptions:
   - **JrGRAS2** improves plant HT tolerance involving in HSP expression.
   - The walnut transcription factor **JrGRAS2** contributes to high temperature stress tolerance involving in Dof transcriptional regulation and HSP protein expression.
   - The similar results were obtained in **JrGRAS2** transient overexpression walnut lines under normal and HT stress conditions.
   - **JrGRAS2** is an useful candidate gene for heat response in plant molecular breeding.
