## Functional Descriptions:
   - Wheat TaSnRK2.10 phosphorylates TaERD15 and **TaENO1** and confers drought tolerance when overexpressed in rice.
   - Phosphorylation of **TaENO1** by TaSnRK2.10 may account for the substantially increased levels of phosphoenolpyruvate (PEP), a key metabolite of primary and secondary metabolism, in TaSnRK2.10-overexpressing rice, thereby enhancing its viability under drought stress. 
   - **TaENO1** is a putative ortholog of AtENO2 which has been reported previously,**TaENO1** encodes an enolase responsible for catalyzing the dehydration of 2-phosphoglyceric acid (2-PGA) to generate PEP, which is an important carbon source and a precursor of multiple metabolic pathways.
