## Functional Descriptions:
   - **TaCHP** encoding C1-domain protein stably enhances wheat yield in saline-alkaline fields.
   - These data suggest that the role of **TaCHP** in salt tolerance does not involve interactions with the phospholipid signaling pathway.
   - **TaCHP** increases wheat yields in saline-alkaline fields, and its genetic variation is associated with saline-alkaline tolerance.
   - Overexpressing **TaCHP** stably enhances wheat yields in saline-alkaline fields without affecting yields under normal conditions, mitigating the trade-off between yield and stress tolerance.
