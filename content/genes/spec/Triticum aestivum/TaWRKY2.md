## Functional Descriptions:
   - Wheat WRKY genes **TaWRKY2** and TaWRKY19 regulate abiotic stress tolerance in transgenic Arabidopsis plants.
   - **TaWRKY2** and TaWRKY19 are nuclear proteins, and displayed specific binding to typical cis-element W box. Transgenic Arabidopsis plants overexpressing **TaWRKY2** exhibited salt and drought tolerance compared with controls.
   - Manipulation of **TaWRKY2** and TaWRKY19 in wheat or other crops should improve their performance under various abiotic stress conditions.
   - **TaWRKY2** and TaWRKY19 may regulate stress tolerance through activation of STZ and DREB2A pathway, respectively.
