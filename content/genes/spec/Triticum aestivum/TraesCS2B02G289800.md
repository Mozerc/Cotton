## Functional Descriptions:
   - TaLBD41 interacts with TaNAC2 to regulate nitrogen uptake and metabolism in response to nitrate availability.
   - The wheat transcription factor TaLBD41 interacts with the nitrate-inducible transcription factor TaNAC2 and is repressed by nitrate provision.
   - Knockdown of TaLBD41 expression enhances N uptake and assimilation, increases spike number, grain yield, and nitrogen harvest index under different N supply conditions.
   - Identified an elite haplotype of **TaLBD41-2B** associated with increased spike number and grain yield.
