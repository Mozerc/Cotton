## Functional Descriptions:
   - A member of wheat class III peroxidase gene family, **TaPRX-2A**, enhanced the tolerance of salt stress.
   - A novel wheat peroxidase (PRX) gene named **TaPRX-2A**, a member of wheat class III PRX gene family, was cloned and its response to salt stress was characterized.
   - The results show that **TaPRX-2A** plays a positive role in the response to salt stress by scavenging ROS and regulating stress-related genes.
   - **TaPRX-2A** regulates ROS scavenging and the expression of stress-related genes in transgenic whea.
