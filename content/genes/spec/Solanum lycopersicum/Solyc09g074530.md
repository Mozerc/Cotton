## Functional Descriptions:
   - Transporter **SlSWEET15** unloads sucrose from phloem and seed coat for fruit and seed development in tomato.
   - Loss of **SlSWEET15** function inhibited fruit and seed development.
   - **SlSWEET15** was involved in vascular sugar transport during fruit development.
   - **SlSWEET15** was the possible candidate sucrose carrier to mediate initial sucrose unloading for fruit expansion and seed filling in tomato.
