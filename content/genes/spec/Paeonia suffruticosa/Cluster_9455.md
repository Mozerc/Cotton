## Functional Descriptions:
   - Tree Peony R2R3-MYB Transcription Factor **PsMYB30** Promotes Petal Blotch Formation by Activating the Transcription of the Anthocyanin Synthase Gene.
   - The R2R3-myeloblastosis (MYB) transcription factor **PsMYB30** belonging to subgroup 1 was proven as a positive anthocyanin regulator with transcriptional activation and nuclear expression.
   - Overexpressing **PsMYB30** increased anthocyanin content by 14.5-fold in tobacco petals.
   - **PsMYB30**, was identified to promote petal blotch formation by activating the expression of PsANS involved in anthocyanin biosynthesis, which provide new insights for petal blotch formation in plants.
