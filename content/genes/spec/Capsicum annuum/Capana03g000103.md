## Functional Descriptions:
   - Involvement of sweet pepper **CaLOX2** in jasmonate-dependent induced defence against Western flower thrips.
   - Our data show that **CaLOX2** is involved in jasmonic acid (JA) biosynthesis and mediates plant resistance.
   - **CaLOX2**-silenced pepper plants exhibited enhanced susceptibility to thrips. This indicates that **CaLOX2** mediates JA-dependent signaling, resulting in defense against thrips.
   - Silencing of **CaLOX2** in pepper plants through virus-induced gene silencing (VIGS) resulted in low levels of **CaLOX2** transcripts, as well as significant reduction in the accumulation of JA, and its derivatives, upon thrips feeding compared to control plants.
