## Functional Descriptions:
   - Pepper stress-associated protein 14 (**CaSAP14**) is a substrate of CaSnRK2.6 that positively modulates abscisic acid-dependent osmotic stress responses.
   - **CaSAP14**-silenced pepper plants showed dehydration- and high salt-sensitive phenotypes, whereas overexpression of **CaSAP14** in Arabidopsis conferred tolerance to dehydration, high salinity, and mannitol treatment, with plants showing ABA-hypersensitive phenotypes.
   - An in-gel kinase assay revealed that CaSnRK2.6 phosphorylates **CaSAP14** in response to exogenous ABA, dehydration, and high-salinity stress. 
   - These findings suggest that **CaSAP14** is a direct substrate of CaSnRK2.6 and positively modulates dehydration- and high salinity-induced osmotic stress responses.
