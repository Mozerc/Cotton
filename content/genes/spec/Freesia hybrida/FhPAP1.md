## Functional Descriptions:
   - The Conserved and Particular Roles of the R2R3-MYB Regulator **FhPAP1** from Freesia hybrida in Flower Anthocyanin Biosynthesis.
   - **FhPAP1** could interact with FhTT8L and FhTTG1 to form the conserved MBW complex and shared similar target genes with its orthologs from Arabidopsis.
   - **FhPAP1** interacts with FhTT8L and FhTTG1 to control anthocyanin biosynthesis in F. hybrida.
   - **FhPAP1** might be the selected gene during the domestication and rapid evolution of the wild Freesia species to generate intensive flower pigmentation.
