## Functional Descriptions:
   - A yeast two-hybrid assay was performed using **PeOST1** as bait and a nucleus-localized factor, JASMONATE ZIM-domain protein 2 (PeJAZ2), was found to participate in MeJA signaling by interacting with **PeOST1**. 
   - PeJAZ and **PeOST1** interact as a putative model for the involvement of MeJA and ABA cross-signaling in drought stress-mediated stomatal movement.
   - The objective was to investigate the role of **PeOST1**, a pivotal factor in ABA signaling, in the regulation of drought tolerance in poplar, and to evaluate the interaction mechanism of JA and ABA in stomatal movement involved in the regulation of drought stress.
