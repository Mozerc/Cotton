## Functional Descriptions:
   - CRISPR/Cas9-mediated mutagenesis of **VvMLO3** results in enhanced resistance to powdery mildew in grapevine.
   - Suggesting that signals from mildew-invaded epidermal cells trigger the collapse of neighboring mesophyll cells as a result of the mutation of **VvMLO3** in these heterozygous lines.
   - Importantly, four **VvMLO3**-edited lines showed enhanced resistance to powdery mildew, which was associated with host cell death, cell wall apposition (CWA) and H2O2 accumulation.
   - **VvMLO3**-edited grapevine plants show infection-triggered cell death, H2O2 accumulation and CWAs.
