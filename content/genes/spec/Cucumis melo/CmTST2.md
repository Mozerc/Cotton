## Functional Descriptions:
   - Overexpression of the tonoplast sugar transporter **CmTST2** in melon fruit increases sugar accumulation.
   - Overexpressing the **CmTST2** gene in strawberry fruit and cucumber plants by transient expression and stable transformation, respectively, both increased sucrose, fructose, and glucose accumulation in the fruit.
   - The **CmTST2**-overexpression construct was transformed into strawberry fruit and cucumber plants to identify the physiological roles of **CmTST2** in plants.
   - The results indicate that **CmTST2** plays an important role in sugar accumulation in melon fruit.
