## Functional Descriptions:
   - **AcERF182** may play an important role in the accumulation of soluble sugar metabolism via direct regulation of the transcription of AcBAM3.5 during fruit ripening of kiwifruit.
   - The expression levels of the key transcription factor **AcERF182** were highly correlated with the sugar accumulation at both harvest ripe and eating ripe stages in all varieties examined
   - **AcERF182** regulates soluble sugar accumulation in kiwifruit.
