## Functional Descriptions:
   - The functional variations in GL15, ZmPHYB2 and **ZmPYL10** that influence kernel row number, flowering time, plant height and ear height.
   - The role of **ZmPYL10** in affecting PH and EH in maize.
   - SVs in **ZmPYL10** for plant height and ear height.
   - ZmPIN7 may be a candidate gene for controlling stalk development in maize. Similarly, AtPYL8 is a homologous gene of **ZmPYL10** in Arabidopsis.
   - To investigate the function of **ZmPYL10** in maize growth and development, we generated a **ZmPYL10** knockout mutant in the ZC01 background using CRISPR/Cas9 technology.
