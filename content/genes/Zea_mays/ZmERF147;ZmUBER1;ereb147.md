   - ZmMYC7 directly regulates **ZmERF147** to increase maize resistance to Fusarium graminearum.
   - These results suggested that **ZmERF147** might be one of the downstream target genes of ZmMYC7.
   - These results showed that the **ZmERF147** gene affected maize resistance to F. graminearum.
