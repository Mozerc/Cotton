## Functional Descriptions:
   - Transcription factors **ZmNF-YA1** and ZmNF-YB16 regulate plant growth and drought tolerance in maize.
   - **ZmNF-YA1** is a positive regulator of plant drought and salt stress responses and is involved in the root development of maize.
   - OE of **ZmNF-YA1** improves plant growth and tolerance to abiotic stresses.
   - **ZmNF-YA1**-mediated transcriptional regulation, especially in JA signaling, histone modification, and chromatin remodeling, could underlie the altered stress tolerance of **ZmNF-YA1** mutant plants.
   - Comparison of ZmNF-YB16- and **ZmNF-YA1**-regulated genes showed that **ZmNF-YA1** and ZmNF-YB16 have similar biological functions in stress responses but varied functions in other biological processes.
   - **ZmNF-YA1** bound to promoter CCAAT motifs and directly regulated the expression of multiple genes that play important roles in stress responses and plant development.
