## Functional Descriptions:
   - In the present study the maize like-aux1 (**Lax1**, GRMZM2G129413) and lax2 (GRMZM2G149481) which encode auxin influx carriers, were down-regulated in rum1. In Arabidopsis, the AUX/LAX gene family members AUX1 and LAX3 act concomitantly in lateral root initiation and emergence.
   - **Lax1** cDNA encoding a protein with a deduced amino acid showing 73% identity to AtAUX1.
   - **Lax1** expresses at a much higher level in foliar leaf primordia (high vein density) than in husk leaf primordia.
   - The auxin signalling-related genes, like-auxin1 (**Lax1**).
   - **Lax1** involved in auxin signal transduction.
