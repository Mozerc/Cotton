## Functional Descriptions:
   - Like other plant disease resistance genes, **Rp1** homologs have been shown to be under divergent or diversifying selection, especially in the LRR2 region.
   - **Rp1** is a complex disease resistance locus in maize that is exceptional in both allelic variability and meiotic instability.
   - **Rp1** encodes a NB-LRR domain containing protein involves in maize rust resistance.
   - Dominant **Rp1** resistant.
