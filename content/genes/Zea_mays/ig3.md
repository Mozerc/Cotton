## Functional Descriptions:
   - **Ig3** mutant produce extra polar nuclei but through defects in embryo sac cellularization rather than through an excess of free nuclear divisions.
   - Consistent with the seed development after pollination of ig2 and **Ig3** mutants with tetraploid males, both mutants can have extra polar nuclei.
   - **Ig3** mutant embryo sacs often have abnormal antipodal cell clusters. The antipodal cells can be fewer in number or absent.
