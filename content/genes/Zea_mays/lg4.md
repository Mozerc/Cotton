## Functional Descriptions:
   - Genes for leaf angle have been cloned mainly using mutants or homology-based cloning method in maize, for instance,lg1, lg2, lg3, and **Lg4**.
   - The dominant mutants of lg3-O and **Lg4**-O showed that the abnormal development of ligular region resulted in a small leaf angle
   - LG2, encoding a basic leucine zipper protein (bZIP); and lg3 and **Lg4**, encoding homeo domain protein.
   - Dominant **Lg4** plants lack ligule and auricle but show vestiges sporadically in blade.
