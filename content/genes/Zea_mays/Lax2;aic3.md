## Functional Descriptions:
   - A part of gene family that maintains auxin distribution pattern against environmental or developmental influences
   - In the present study the maize like-aux1 (lax1, GRMZM2G129413) and **Lax2** (GRMZM2G149481) which encode auxin influx carriers, were down-regulated in rum1. In Arabidopsis, the AUX/LAX gene family members AUX1 and LAX3 act concomitantly in lateral root initiation and emergence.
   - The auxin signalling-related genes, like-auxin2 (**Lax2**).
   - **Lax2** involved in auxin signal transduction.
