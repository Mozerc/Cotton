## Functional Description:
   - The expression patterns of six key NRTs genes (NPF6.6, NPF6.8, NRT2.1, NRT2.5 and NRT3.1A/B) varied in response to alterations in nitrogen levels.
   - NRT2.4 and NRT3.1B responded to ABA.
   - **ZmNRT3.1B** are higher during the pollination period than before, and their expression in leaves are considerably higher than that in roots.
