## Functional Descriptions:
   - The closest homologue of maize **Lbd24** is Arabidopsis LBD16. AtLBD16 controls asymmetric division of lateral root founder cells in Arabidopsis, which is directly regulated by the SLR/IAA14-ARF7/ARF19-dependent auxin signalling module which controls Arabidopsis lateral root initiation.
   - Maize **Lbd24** controls asymmetric division of lateral root founder cells.
   - Maize **Lbd24** (GRMZM2G075499) was down-regulated more than 4-fold in rum1 primary roots.
