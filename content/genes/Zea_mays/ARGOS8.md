## Functional Descriptions:
   - Maize **ARGOS8** is a negative regulator of ethylene responses. A previous study has shown that transgenic plants constitutively overexpressing **ARGOS8** have reduced ethylene sensitivity and improved grain yield under drought stress conditions.
   - The **ARGOS8** variants increased grain yield by five bushels per acre under flowering stress conditions and had no yield loss under well-watered conditions.
   - Maize **ARGOS8** is a negative regulator of ethylene responses.
   - Overexpressing **ARGOS8** have reduced ethylene sensitivity and improved grain yield under drought stress conditions.
   - **ARGOS8** reduced nodal roots.
