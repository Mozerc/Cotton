## Functional Descriptions:
   - Zmcyc1 and **Zmcyc5** were expressed in all studied tissues with the highest expression in leaves and lowest expression in roots.
   - Wounding, methyl jasmonate and salicylic acid significantly induced the expression of Zmcyc1 and **Zmcyc5** genes.
   - Zmcyc1 and **Zmcyc5** encoding PR proteins could also play defense roles in the maize defense systems against fungi.
