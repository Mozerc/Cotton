## Functional Description:
   - **ZmWEE1** kinase phosphorylates ZmTE1, thus confining it to the nucleus and probably reducing cell division.
   - **ZmWEE1** and ZmPP2Ac-2 might balance ZmTE1 activity, controlling cell division and elongation to maintain normal maize growth.
   - ZmPP2Ac-2 and **ZmWEE1** may regulate phosphorylation and subcellular localization of ZmTE1, thus impacting cell division, cell elongation, and plant height.
