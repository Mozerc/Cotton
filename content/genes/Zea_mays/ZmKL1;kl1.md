## Functional Descriptions:
   - Regulation analyses through expression quantitative trait locus mapping revealed that cis-regulatory variations likely control kernel size by fine-tuning the expression of proximal genes, among which **ZmKL1** (GRMZM2G098305) was transgenically validated.
   - These findings support the notion that **ZmKL1** is positively associated with kernel size in maize.
   - **ZmKL1** positively associated with maize kernel size through cis-regulatory variants.
