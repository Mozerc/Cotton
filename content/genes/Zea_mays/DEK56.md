## Functional Descriptions:
   - Characterized a mutant of DEFECTIVE KERNEL 56 (**DEK56**) with defective kernels that exhibited arrested development of both the embryo and endosperm.
   - Dysfunction of **DEK56** resulted in altered cytidine (C)-to-uridine (U) editing efficiency at 48 editing sites across 21 mitochondrial transcripts.
   - DEFECTIVE KERNEL 56 functions in mitochondrial RNA editing and maize seed development.
   - **DEK56** encodes a mitochondria-targeted E subgroup PPR protein.
   - **DEK56** exhibited an irregular mitochondrion with a collapsed inner structure.
