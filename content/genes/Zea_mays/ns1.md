## Functional Descriptions:
   - The maize gene NARROWSHEATH1 (**Ns1**) is a WUSCHEL-related homeobox3 (WOX3) homolog expressed at the margins of leaf primordia, and is required for mediolateral outgrowth.
   - Microscopic analyses of cell division and gene expression in expanding leaves, and reverse genetic analyses of homologous **Ns1** target genes in Arabidopsis, reveal that **Ns1** controls mediolateral outgrowth by repression of a growth inhibitor and promotion of cell division at primordial leaf margins.
   - **Ns1** controls mediolateral outgrowth by repression of a growth inhibitor and promotion of cell division at primordial leaf margins.
   - **Ns1** function activates cell division and growth from leaf margins.
   - **Ns1** overexpression promotes abnormal growth in leaf margins.
