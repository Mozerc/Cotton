## Functional Descriptions:
   - ZmCOI1a-**ZmJAZ15** Action Module in Regulating Maize Immunity to Gibberella Stalk Rot.
   - **ZmJAZ15** plays a pivotal role, whereas ZmCOIa and endogenous JA itself might function as susceptibility factors, in maize immunity to GSR.
   - **ZmJAZ15** played a negative role in the resistance to GSR.
