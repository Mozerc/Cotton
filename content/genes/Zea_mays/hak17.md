## Functional Descriptions:
   - In YZ26, three genes for Na+/K+ homeostasis, including H+-ATPase8, HAK5, and **HAK17** were differentially expressed in salt-treated samples when compared with controls.
   - Two HAK members HAK5 and **HAK17** for K+ transport were upregulated in salt-treated YZ26 relative to normal counterpart.
   - **HAK17** encodes a potassium high-affinity transporter.
