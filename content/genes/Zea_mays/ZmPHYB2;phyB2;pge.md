   - The functional variations in GL15, **ZmPHYB2** and ZmPYL10 that influence kernel row number, flowering time, plant height and ear height.
   - Together, these results suggest that Hap4 of **ZmPHYB2** is likely to represent a favorable haplotype under selection for early flowering during modern maize breeding.
