## Functional Description:
   - The **ZmCRY1b** transgene product can activate the light signaling pathway in Arabidopsis and complement the etiolation phenotype of the cry1-304 mutant.
   - Overexpression of **ZmCRY1b** in maize substantially inhibits seedling etiolation.
   - **ZmCRY1b** contributes blue-light signaling upon seedling de-etiolation and integrates light signals with the GA metabolic pathway in maize, resulting in lodging resistance and providing information for improving maize varieties.
   - The **ZmCRY1b** transgene can significantly reduce maize plant height under different light conditions in the field
