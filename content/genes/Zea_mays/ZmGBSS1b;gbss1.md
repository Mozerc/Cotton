## Functional Descriptions:
   - We identified gene involved in carbohydrate metabolism, including Zm00001eb282250 and **ZmGBSS1b**.
   - **ZmGBSS1b** is homologous to Arabidopsis UDP-glycosyltransferase gene AtGBSS, which was involved in transitory starch synthesis.
   - **ZmGBSS1b**, ZmGLN1, ZmXYL4, and ZmHB41 may be involved in regulating grain production and carbohydrates/N assimilates transport.
   - **ZmGBSS1b**, ZmHB41, and ZmXYL4 may also play key roles in C metabolism and the transport of carbohydrate/N assimilates, affecting grain production in response to N availability.
