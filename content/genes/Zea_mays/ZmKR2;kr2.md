   - Functional modification of these hydrocarbon scaffolds through cytochrome P450 monooxygenases (P450s) of the CYP701 (ZmCYP701A43) and CYP71 (ZmCYP71Z16, ZmCYP71Z18) families, along with a steroid 5Î±-reductase termed Kauralexin Reductase 2 (**ZmKR2**), then largely defines the structural diversity and bioactivity of KX and DX metabolites.
   - One known diterpenoid pathway gene, **ZmKR2**, was differentially expressed between WT and Zmksl4 under both well-watered and water-deficit conditions. None of the known GA-biosynthesis genes were significantly differentially expressed between WT and Zmksl4 under either water condition.
