## Functional Descriptions:
   - Overexpressing **ZCN1**, ZCN2, ZCN4, and ZCN5 maintained the indeterminacy of the inflorescence meristem for a longer period, allowing the production of more lateral organs compared with nontransgenic siblings.
   - **ZCN1** showed moderate effects on flowering time and tassel morphology.
   - Overexpression of **ZCN1** produced transgenic plants that are late flowering and have tassels with an increased number of lateral branches and high spikelet density. 
   - Tassel and ear traits of **ZCN1** to -6 transgenic maize plants.
