## Functional Descriptions:
   - **ZmTPS1** Contributed to the Metabolic Divergence in the Terpenoid Pathway.
   - **ZmTPS1** could catalyze the formation of linalool in vitro.
   - **ZmTPS1** a gene encoding a terpene synthase, is among the most likely candidates for the mQTL on chromosome 2.
   - A previous study has shown that **ZmTPS1** could catalyze the formation of linalool in vitro.
   - **ZmTPS1** is important contributor to linalool and β-myrcene emission; aids host location in the chewing insect S. frugiperda, yet repels the sap-sucking pest, Rhopalosiphum maidis.
   - **ZmTPS1** possible plant defense gene
