## Functional Descriptions:
   - Transcription factors ZmNF-YA1 and **ZmNF-YB16** regulate plant growth and drought tolerance in maize.
   - OE of ZmNF-YA1 or **ZmNF-YB16** produces similar effects on abiotic stress tolerance of maize.
   - OE of **ZmNF-YB16** improves drought tolerance and yield by enhancing photosynthesis and the antioxidant capacity of maize plants.
   - In this work, we used yeast two-hybrid screening to identify potential interactors of **ZmNF-YB16** and confirmed the interaction between ZmNF-YA1 and **ZmNF-YB16**-YC17 and between ZmNF-YA7 and **ZmNF-YB16**-YC17.
   - **ZmNF-YB16** interacted with ZmNF-YC17 via its histone fold domain to form a heterodimer in the cytoplasm and then entered the nucleus to form a heterotrimer with ZmNF-YA1 or ZmNF-YA7 under osmotic stress.
   - Comparison of **ZmNF-YB16**- and ZmNF-YA1-regulated genes showed that ZmNF-YA1 and **ZmNF-YB16** have similar biological functions in stress responses but varied functions in other biological processes.
