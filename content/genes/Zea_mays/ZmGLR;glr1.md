## Functional Description:
   - **ZmGLR**, a cell membrane localized microtubule-associated protein, mediated leaf morphogenesis in maize.
   - The N-terminal 24 amino acids and Ca2+ affect the membrane localization of **ZmGLR**.
   - **ZmGLR** is involved in the brassinolide and auxin signaling pathways.
