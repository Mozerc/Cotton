## Functional Description:
   - **ZmCKX10** encodes a cytokinin dehydrogenase and is downregulated by low N in leaves, which may be involved in leaf senescence and ASI in response to N availability.
   - **ZmCKX10** encoding cytokinin dehydrogenase and an auxin-responsive gene.
