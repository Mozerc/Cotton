## Functional Description:
   - **Smk501** encodes a RUBylation activating enzyme E1 subunit ZmECR1 (E1 C-TERMINAL RELATED 1) protein.
   - **Smk501** gene, revealed its molecular function, and defined its roles in RUBylation pathway and seed development.
   - **Smk501** knockdown affects cell proliferation and hormone homeostasis.
