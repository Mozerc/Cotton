## Functional Descriptions:
   - **ZmEMF1L1** was associated with three agronomic traits: days to anthesis, ear height and relative ear height. It encodes a protein homologous to the Arabidopsis EMBRYONIC FLOWER1 (EMF1) protein, which is involved in the control of flowering time and plant architecture.
   - It encodes a protein homologous to the Arabidopsis EMBRYONIC FLOWER1 (EMF1) protein, which is involved in the control of flowering time and plant architecture, and thus was named Zea mays EMF1-like 1 (**ZmEMF1L1**).
