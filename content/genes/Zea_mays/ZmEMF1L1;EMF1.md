## Functional Description:
   - **Zm**EMF1**L1** was associated with three agronomic traits: days to anthesis, ear height and relative ear height. It encodes a protein homologous to the Arabidopsis EMBRYONIC FLOWER1 (**EMF1**) protein, which is involved in the control of flowering time and plant architecture.
