## Functional Description:
   - The maize heterotrimeric G protein α subunit COMPACT PLANT2 (**Ct2**) functions with CLV receptors to regulate meristem development.
   - **Ct2** and XLGs have both redundant and specialized functions in regulating shoot development. Importantly, we show that a constitutively active Gα functioned as a weak allele, which introduced multiple desirable agronomic traits, such as improved kernel row number and reduced leaf angle.
