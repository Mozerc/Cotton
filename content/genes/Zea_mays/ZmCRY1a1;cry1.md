## Functional Description:
   - Zm**cry1**a has a relatively conservative function in regulating maize photomorphogenesis.
   - **Zm**cry1**a1** and Zm**cry1**a2 caused short mesocotyl and first leaf sheath lengths in maize under blue and white light
   - Overexpression of **Zm**cry1**a1** and Zm**cry1**a2 reduced maize plant height and ear height
