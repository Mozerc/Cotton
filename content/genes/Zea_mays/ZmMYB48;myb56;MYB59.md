## Functional Description:
   - We hypothesized that brassinolide could induce the overexpression of **ZmMYB48** gene under moderate and severe drought stress, thus activating a series of downstream cascade reactions, improving the photosynthetic performance, antioxidant performance, proline content and a series of indicators of maize seedlings, thus improving the drought tolerance of maize seedlings.
   - **ZmMYB48** induced by drought and ABA treatments.
   - Overexpression of **ZmMYB48** gene under drought stress can improve the drought tolerance of plants.
