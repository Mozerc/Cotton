## Functional Description:
   - **ZmPR1** is a SA-responsive gene, encodes a defense-related protein.
   - The expression levels of SA-associated genes **ZmPR1**a and ZmPR5 exhibited significant increases in the ZmERF105 over-expression lines but were decreased in the erf105 mutant lines after E. turcicum infection.
