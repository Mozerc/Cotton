## Functional Descriptions:
   - It was found that **ZmBON3** may be involved in the related defense processes mediated by the R gene, SA pathway, JA pathway, and ABA pathway.
   - The disease resistance of gene-edited plants was significantly increased, which verified that the **ZmBON3** gene was a negative regulatory gene.
   - **ZmBON3**-geneedited plants showed obvious dwarf phenomenon at the seedling stage, but this did not affect the ear length, axis diameter, ear row number, and grain color.
