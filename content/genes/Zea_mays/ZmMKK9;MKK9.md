## Functional Description:
   - **Zm**MKK9**** and ZmRIN2 play negative regulatory roles in high-temperature–induced stomatal opening.
   - The **Zm**MKK9****–ZmMPK20–ZmRIN2 cascade negatively regulates high-temperature–induced stomatal opening and balances water loss and leaf temperature, thus enhancing plant thermotolerance.
