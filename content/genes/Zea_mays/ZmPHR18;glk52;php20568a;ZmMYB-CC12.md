## Functional Descriptions:
   - **ZmPHR18** and two (ZmPhr5 and ZmPhr10) genes are up-and down-regulated in leaf, respectively, indicating their probable involvement in the regulation of Pi translocation in leaves under different Pi level. 
   - **ZmPHR18** involved plants show a remarkable adaptation response to low-Pi stress
   - **ZmPHR18** involved in phosphate starvation response.
