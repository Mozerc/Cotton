## Functional Descriptions:
   - **ZmSnRK1a2** gene product is sucrose non-fermenting-1-related kinase.
   - **ZmSnRK1a2** Phosphorylates ZmRFWD3 at Ser-479 To Regulate Its Stability.
   - The activity of O2 responds to Suc levels through the ZmSnRK1-ZmRFWD3-O2 signaling axis.
