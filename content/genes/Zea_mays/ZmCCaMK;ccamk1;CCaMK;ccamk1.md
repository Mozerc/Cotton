## Functional Description:
   - ZmBSK1 localizes in plasma membrane, interacts with calcium/calmodulin (Ca2+/CaM)-dependent protein kinase (**Zm**CCaMK****), and phosphorylates **Zm**CCaMK****. Ser-67 is a crucial phosphorylation site of **Zm**CCaMK**** by ZmBSK1.
   - Drought stress enhances not only the interaction between ZmBSK1 and **Zm**CCaMK**** but also the phosphorylation of Ser-67 in **Zm**CCaMK**** by ZmBSK1. Furthermore, Ser-67 phosphorylation in **Zm**CCaMK**** regulates its Ca2+/CaM binding, autophosphorylation and transphosphorylation activity, and positively affects its function in drought tolerance in maize.
   - In maize, **Zm**CCaMK**** phosphorylating NAC84 regulates ABA-induced antioxidant defense and drought stress.
