## Functional Description:
   - **ZmGS1-5** is a cytosolic GS1-5 isoform, a major species in root; gene specific cDNA probe, 6-member nuclear gene family.
   - **ZmGS1-5** mRNA has highest accumulation in root cortex and stem, moderate accumulation in seedling shoot, no detectable accumulation in leaf blade, young cob, embryo, pollen, root tip, root vascular cylinder.
   - **ZmGS1-5** encodes a glutamate-ammonia ligase, cytosol.
