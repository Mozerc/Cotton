## Functional Descriptions:
   - The maize CRABS CLAW co-orthologs drooping leaf1 (drl1) and **Drl2** are required for development of ear and tassel florets.
   - Natural variation and transposon alleles of **Drl2** enhance drl1 mutant phenotypes by reducing floral meristem (FM) determinacy.
   - Drooping leaf1 (drl1) and **Drl2** are required for development of ear and tassel florets.
   - Drl1 and **Drl2** regulate floret development.
   - Drl1 and **Drl2** impose floral meristem determinacy.
   - Drl1 and **Drl2** mutant enhances the phenotype of ifa1 mutants.
