## Functional Description:
   - **ZmICK7** interacts with D-type cyclins. Transformed and expressed ZmCDKA1 and ZmICKs together in fission yeast revealed that ZmICK1, ZmICK3, and ZmICK4 can affect ZmCDKA1 function.
   - ZmICK5, ZmICK6, and **ZmICK7** are closely related, whereas ZmICK8 is unique compared with the other seven ZmICK genes. ZmICK8 also has unique characteristics in terms of genetic structure and conserved motifs.
   - ZmICK6 and **ZmICK7** might be involved in the development of the maize endosperm and seed filling.
