## Functional Descriptions:
   - **ZmMs33** in controlling male sterility and JA-mediated stress response in maize.
   - **ZmMs33** is indispensable for the development and function of anther chloroplasts, localized mainly in the endothecium layer of the anther wall.
   - **ZmMs33** in stress response during anther development.
