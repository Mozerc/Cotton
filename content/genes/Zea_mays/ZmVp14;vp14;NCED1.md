## Functional Description:
   - The **Zm**vp14**** gene was characterized from the ABA-deficient maize mutant **vp14** and is known to be regulated by salinity and osmotic stress
   - Transient alkalinization of the leaf apoplast increases mRNA abundance of viviparous 14 (**vp14**).
   - The present study demonstrates for the first time that the stomatal closure, elicited by the apoplastic alkalinization, is also due to a pHapo-mediated increase in the transcription of the NCED gene **vp14** in the leaf. **Zm**vp14**** encodes the rate-limiting enzyme in ABA biosynthesis.
