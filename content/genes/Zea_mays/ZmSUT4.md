## Functional Descriptions:
   - ZmSWEET14a/15a and **ZmSUT4** are responsible for sucrose transport from the periembryonic region (endosperm) to the embryo, respectively.
   - **ZmSUT4** was constitutively expressed throughout the growth period of grain, but the temporal expression of ZmSTP4 and ZmSWEET6b was not observed.
   - ZmSWEET14a/15a may be responsible for efflux of sucrose from the ESR, followed by uptake into embryos by **ZmSUT4**.
