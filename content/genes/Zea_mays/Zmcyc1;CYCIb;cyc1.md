## Functional Description:
   - **Zm**cyc1**** and Zmcyc5 were expressed in all studied tissues with the highest expression in leaves and lowest expression in roots.
   - Wounding, methyl jasmonate and salicylic acid significantly induced the expression of **Zm**cyc1**** and Zmcyc5 genes.
   - **Zm**cyc1**** and Zmcyc5 encoding PR proteins could also play defense roles in the maize defense systems against fungi.
