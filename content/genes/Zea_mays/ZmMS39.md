## Functional Descriptions:
   - A novel male-sterile maize mutant male sterility 39 (**ZmMS39**) was obtained from offspring of the commercial hybrid Chuandan No. 9 that had been carried into outer space.
   - **ZmMS39** is controlled by a single recessive nuclear gene, located on the long arm of chromosome 3.
   - **ZmMS39** encodes a callose synthase essential for male fertility in maize.
   - Mutant is male sterile and also exhibits a shorter plant height and tassel length and decreased tassel branch number.
