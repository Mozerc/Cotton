## Functional Descriptions:
   - Real-time PCR analysis revealed **ZmTAC1** expression was the highest in the leaf-sheath pulvinus, less in the leaf and shoot apical meristem, and the lowest in the root.
   - **ZmTAC1** had been extensively utilized in breeding with regard to the improvement of the maize plant architecture.
   - **ZmTAC1** expression might contribute to leaf angle polymorphism.
   - **ZmTAC1** might play an important role in the leaf regulatory pathway.
   - **ZmTAC1** is involved in the mRNA level and leaf development in maize.
