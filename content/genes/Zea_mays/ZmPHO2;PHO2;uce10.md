## Functional Descriptions:
   - Knockout of **ZmPHO2** (Phosphate 2), a miR399 target, resulted in a similar premature senescence phenotype.
   - Our study reveals a direct functional link between Pi-deprivation sensing by the miR399-**ZmPHO2** regulatory module and plant developmental regulation by ID1.
   - INDETERMINATE1 autonomously regulates phosphate homeostasis upstream of the miR399-**ZmPHO2** signaling module in maize.
   - **ZmPHO2** was under selection during maize domestication and cultivation, resulting in a more sensitive response to Pi starvation in temperate maize than in tropical maize.
   - Knocking out **ZmPHO2**, a miR399 target, results in premature senescence.
   - **ZmPHO2** ortholog of Arabidopsis Ubiquitin-conjugating E2 family protein that is involved in phosphate starvation response.
