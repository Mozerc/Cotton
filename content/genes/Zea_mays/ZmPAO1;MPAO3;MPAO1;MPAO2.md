## Functional Descriptions:
   - RNA-sequencing coupled with gene expression assays revealed that both ZmARF3 and ZmARF30 directly bind the promoter of Polyamine Oxidase 1 (**ZmPAO1**) and activate its expression.
   - We uncovered a role of the Zma-miR167-ZmARF3/30 module for restricting MCMV infection by regulating **ZmPAO1** expression, while MCMV employs p31 to counteract this defense.
   - Knockdown or inhibition of enzymatic activity of **ZmPAO1** suppressed MCMV infection. Nevertheless.
   - Silencing of **ZmPAO1** transcripts or inhibiting ZmPAO activity suppressed MCMV infection.
   - MCMV-encoded p31 protein directly targets and enhances **ZmPAO1** enzyme activity.
   - **ZmPAO1** plays an important role in tumor development during the maize-U.
