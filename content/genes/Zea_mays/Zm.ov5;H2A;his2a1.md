## Functional Descriptions:
   - The cDNA clones **Zm.ov5** and Zm.ov1 showed homology to histones H2A and H2B, respectively, and computer alignments of the putative amino acid sequences of these two clones showed that they represent expressed variants of histones H2A and H2B.
   - **H2A** cDNA from unfertilized ovule, similar to histone2A.
