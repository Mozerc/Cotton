## Functional Descriptions:
   - **ZmNAC16** and ZmSBP18 were associated with leaf angle.
   - **ZmNAC84** is a gene in response to drought and other osmotic stresses.
   - **ZmNAC84** displays a partially overlapping expression pattern with ZmCCaMK after ABA treatment, and H2O2 is required for ABA-induced **ZmNAC84** expression.
   - Functional analysis reveals that **ZmNAC84** is essential for ABA-induced antioxidant defense in a ZmCCaMK-dependent manner. Furthermore, ZmCCaMK directly phosphorylates Ser-113 of **ZmNAC84** in vitro, and Ser-113 is essential for the ABA-induced stimulation of antioxidant defense by ZmCCaMK.
