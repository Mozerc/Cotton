## Functional Description:
   - **CsPrx25**, a class III peroxidase in Citrus sinensis, confers resistance to citrus bacterial canker through the maintenance of ROS homeostasis and cell wall lignification.
   - The role of **CsPrx25** and its contribution to plant defenses in molecular detail. Based on the expression analysis.
   - **CsPrx25** overexpression in sweet orange induces resistance to CBC.
