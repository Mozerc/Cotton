## Functional Descriptions:
   - **CsbZIP44** is essential for ABA-mediated carotenoid biosynthesis in citrus.
   - The transcriptional regulatory module CsHB5-**CsbZIP44** positively regulates abscisic acid-mediated carotenoid biosynthesis in citrus.
   - A novel bZIP transcription activator called **CsbZIP44**, which serves as a central regulator of ABA-mediated citrus carotenoid biosynthesis.
