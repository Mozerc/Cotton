## Functional Descriptions:
   - **TaABI19** positively regulates grain development in wheat.
   - **TaABI19** is highly expressed in young spikes and developing grains, and encodes a nucleus-localized transcriptional activator in wheat.
   - **TaABI19** could bind to the promoters of TaPBF homologous genes and enhance their expression. Haplotype association showed that **TaABI19-B1** is significantly associated with TGW.
