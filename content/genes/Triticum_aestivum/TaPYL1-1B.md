## Functional Descriptions:
   - The wheat ABA receptor gene **TaPYL1-1B** contributes to drought tolerance and grain yield by increasing water-use efficiency.
   - **TaPYL1-1B**, exhibited higher ABA sensitivity, photosynthetic capacity and water-use efficiency (WUE), all contributed to higher drought tolerance than that of wild-type plants.
   - Our findings illuminate the role of **TaPYL1-1B** plays in coordinating drought tolerance and grain yield.
   - **TaPYL1-1B** in improving wheat drought tolerance and grain yield.
   - **TaPYL1-1B** overexpression confers drought tolerance.
   - **TaPYL1-1B** overexpression increases water-use efficiency.
