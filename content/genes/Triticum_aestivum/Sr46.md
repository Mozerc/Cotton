## Functional Descriptions:
   - This gene, hereafter referred to as **Sr46**, encodes a coiled-coil NLR protein that conferred stem rust resistance when expressed as a transgene in the susceptible wheat.
   - **Sr46** promotes stem rust resistance in wheats.
