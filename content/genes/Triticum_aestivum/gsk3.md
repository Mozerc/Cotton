## Functional Descriptions:
   - GLYCOGEN SYNTHASE KINASE 3 (**GSK3**) physically interacts with VRN1 and regulates its accumulation to mediate flowering in wheat.
   - These results indicated that the over-accumulated **GSK3** protein readily promotes the degradation of VRN1 in the gain-of-function **GSK3** mutant.
   - Our study provides direct evidence that **GSK3**, the core kinase in the BR signaling pathway, regulates flowering in wheat by modulating the protein stability of VRN1.
