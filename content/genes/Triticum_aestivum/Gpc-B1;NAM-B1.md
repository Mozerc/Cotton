## Functional Descriptions:
   - We report here the positional cloning of **Gpc-B1**, a wheat quantitative trait locus associated with increased grain protein.
   - Complete linkage of the 7.4-kb region with the different phenotypes suggests that **Gpc-B1** is a single gene with multiple pleiotropic effects.
   - The ancestral wild wheat allele encodes a NAC transcription factor (**NAM-B1**) that accelerates senescence and increases nutrient remobilization from leaves to developing grains, whereas modern wheat varieties carry a nonfunctional **NAM-B1** allele.
   - Reduction in RNA levels of the multiple NAM homologs by RNA interference delayed senescence by more than 3 weeks and reduced wheat grain protein, zinc, and iron content by more than 30%.
