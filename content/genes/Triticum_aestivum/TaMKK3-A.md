## Functional Descriptions:
   - Identified a mitogen-activated protein kinase kinase 3 (MKK3) gene (designated **TaMKK3-A**) by a map-based approach as a candidate gene for the seed dormancy locus Phs1 on chromosome 4A in bread wheat.
   - Complementation analysis showed that transformation of a dormant wheat cultivar with the **TaMKK3-A** allele from a nondormant cultivar clearly reduced seed dormancy.
   - **TaMKK3-A** gene expression, measured at the transcript level by RT-PCR, was detected not only in seeds but also in other tissues such as leaves, cotyledons, and roots
   - Relative gene expression and dormancy level were correlated, therefore, higher **TaMKK3-A** gene expression may promote dormancy release.
