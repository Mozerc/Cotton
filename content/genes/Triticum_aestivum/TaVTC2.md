## Functional Descriptions:
   - **TaVTC2** positively regulates WYMV infection in wheat.
   - **TaVTC2** scavenges ROS accumulation promoting viral infection in planta.
   - NIb interferes with the function of **TaVTC2** in ROS scavenging in a dosage-dependent manner.
   - Wheat yellow mosaic virus NIb targets **TaVTC2** to elicit broad-spectrum pathogen resistance in wheat.
   - **TaVTC2** RNAi plants conferred broad-spectrum disease resistance.
   - The data indicate that **TaVTC2** recruits WYMV NIb to down-regulate its own enzymatic activity, reducing AsA accumulation to elicit a burst of ROS which confers the resistance to WYMV infection.
