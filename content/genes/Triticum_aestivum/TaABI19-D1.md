## Functional Descriptions:
   - Cloned a B3 family TF in wheat, named **TaABI19**, based on its orthologous sequence in maize.
   - **TaABI19** positively regulates grain development in wheat.
   - That the haplotypes of **TaABI19** were significantly associated with TGW in wheat.
   - **TaABI19** is mainly expressed in developing grain.
   - **TaABI19** still can be considered as a target locus for marker-assisted selection breeding to increase TGW in China.
