## Functional Descriptions:
   - We detected a polymorphic DNA fragment in the line with the **Lr10** resistance gene.This fragment mapped to the **Lr10** disease resistance locus and encodes a receptor-like protein kinase which we called LRK10.
   - Molecular cloning of a new receptor-like kinase gene encoded at the **Lr10** disease resistance locus of wheat.
   - Analysis of wheat lines with and without the **Lr10** gene demonstrated that Lrk10 and **Lr10** belong to the same genetic locus.
