## Functional Descriptions:
   - TaABF2–TaERF87/TaAKS1–TaP5CS1/TaP5CR1 module, and identifies candidate genes for the development of elite drought-tolerant wheat varieties.
   - TaP5CR1 expression are likely responsible for the respective effects on drought tolerance or sensitivity observed in the pUbi:TaERF87 OE and BSMV:TaERF87 plants.
   - RNA sequencing with biochemical assays revealed that TaERF87 activates the expression of the proline biosynthesis genes TaP5CS1 and TaP5CR1 via direct binding to GCC-box elements.
   - These findings showed that TaERF87 is a transcriptional activator of TaP5CS1s and TaP5CR1s expression, and confirmed that TaP5CS1-A promotes proline biosynthesis and enhances wheat tolerance to drought stress.
   - Coordination of TaAKS1 and TaERF87 has additive effects on TaP5CS1s and TaP5CR1s activation.
