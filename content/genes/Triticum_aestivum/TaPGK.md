## Functional Descriptions:
   - Global crotonylatome and GWAS revealed a TaSRT1-**TaPGK** model regulating wheat cold tolerance through mediating pyruvate.
   - We demonstrated the TaSRT1-**TaPGK** model regulating wheat cold stress, possibly by promoting pyruvate accumulation.
   - **TaPGK** positively modulating wheat cold tolerance.
   - Lysine crotonylation of **TaPGK** induced by cold stress.
   - Transcriptome sequencing indicated that overexpression of **TaPGK** activated glycolytic key genes and thereby increased pyruvate content.
