## Functional Description:
   - This study reveals new functional roles of **TaSTT3b-2B** in regulating plant innate immunity and grain weight
   - Overexpression of **TaSTT3b-2B** boosted wheat resistance to R. cerealis
   - RNA-seq analysis identifies defense-related genes modulated by **TaSTT3b-2B**
   - Overexpression of **TaSTT3b-2B** increased grain size in transgenic wheat with R. cerealis infection
