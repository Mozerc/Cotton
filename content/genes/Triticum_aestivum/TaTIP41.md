## Functional Description:
   - **TaTIP41** and TaTAP46 positively regulate drought tolerance in wheat by inhibiting PP2A activity
   - **TaTIP41** acts as a positive regulator of wheat drought tolerance
   - **TaTIP41** positively regulates the ABA response to promote ABA-mediated stomatal closure
   - Both **TaTIP41** and TaTAP46 inhibit PP2A activity via their interaction with PP2A catalytic subunits
