## Functional Descriptions:
   - Improvement of wheat drought tolerance through editing of TaATX4 by CRISPR/Cas9.
   - TaATX4 knockout improves drought tolerance in wheat.
   - TaATX4 performs a major role in negatively regulating wheat drought tolerance by orchestrating the transcriptional response to drought stress.
   - Treatment with exogenous abscisic acid (ABA) or 20% PEG induced the transcriptional expression of TaATX4 homoeologs, which gradually increased with prolonged treatment time
   - The simultaneous triple knockout (KO) of all three TaATX4 homoeologs in wheat to observe the effects on drought tolerance.
