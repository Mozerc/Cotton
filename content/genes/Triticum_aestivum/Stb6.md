## Functional Descriptions:
   - Here we report the map-based cloning of the wheat **Stb6** gene and demonstrate that it encodes a conserved wall-associated receptor kinase (WAK)-like protein, which detects the presence of a matching apoplastic effector6,7,8 and confers pathogen resistance without a hypersensitive response.
   - Wheat receptor-kinase-like protein **Stb6** controls gene-for-gene resistance to fungal pathogen Zymoseptoria tritici.
   - **Stb6** is, to our knowledge, the first cloned gene specifying resistance to Zymoseptoria tritici.
