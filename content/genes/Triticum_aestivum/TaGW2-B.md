## Functional Descriptions:
   - The simultaneous silencing of TaDA1 and TaGW2 has an additive effect on kernel size and weight.
   - TaDA1 and TaGW2 function in partially overlapping but relatively independent protein networks.
   - TaDA1-A physically interacted with **TaGW2-B** and verified their additive effects not only through the enhanced kernel size phenotype generated by the simultaneous down-regulation of TaDA1 and TaGW2 in wheat, but also using a combinational analysis of TaDA1-A and **TaGW2-B** haplotypes in the association population.
   - Demonstrate the presence of an additive effect of the TaDA1-A and **TaGW2-B** haplotypes on the regulation of TKW.
