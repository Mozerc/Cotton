## Functional Descriptions:
   - Identification of **TaGL1-B1** gene controlling grain length through regulation of jasmonic acid in common wheat.
   - The gene **TaGL1-B1** encoding carotenoid isomerase was identified in a secondary large population through multiple strategies.
   - EMS mutants of **TaGL1** possessed significantly reduced grain length, whereas TaGL1-B1-overexpressed lines possessed significantly increased grain length.
   - Taken together, overexpression of **TaGL1-B1** enhanced grain length, probably through interaction with TaPAP6 to cause the accumulation of JA that improved carotenoid content and photosynthesis, thereby resulted in increased wheat yield.
   - Mutant and overexpression revealed the key role of **TaGL1-B1** regulating wheat carotenoid and chlorophyll contents.
