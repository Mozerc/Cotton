## Functional Descriptions:
   - The present study aimed to clone the homoeologous genes (homoeoalleles) for Rec8-like cohesin in polyploid wheat.
   - We cloned two Rec8-like homoeoalleles from tetraploid wheat (TtRec8-A1 and TtRec8-B1) and one from hexaploid wheat (TaRec8-D1), and performed expression and functional analyses of the homoeoalleles.
   - **TaRec8-A1** is one of the homoeologous genes for the Rec8-like meiotic cohesin in polyploid wheat.
