## Functional Descriptions:
   - **TaNF-YB1** interacted with TabHLH95 to enhance its regulatory activities on starch synthesis.
   - TabHLH95-**TaNF-YB1** coregulates the transcription of TaAGPL2, TaAGPL1, and TaISA1.
   - The interaction between **TaNF-YB1** and TabHLH95 may improve the efficiency of starch synthesis in wheat endosperm.
