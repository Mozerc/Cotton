## Functional Descriptions:
   - **Lr14a** confers leaf rust resistance in ArinaLrFor.
   - **Lr14a** encodes a membrane-localized protein containing twelve ankyrin (ANK) repeats and structural similarities to Ca2+-permeable non-selective cation channels. 
   - Transcriptome analyses reveal an induction of genes associated with calcium ion binding in the presence of **Lr14a**.
   - Of these three genes, only **Lr14a** confers seedling resistance at ambient temperatures.
   - These unique properties of phenotypic and genetic interactions of a major seedling disease resistance gene make **Lr14a** an interesting target for molecular functional analyses.
