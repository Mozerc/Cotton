## Functional Descriptions:
   - Transcription factors **GmERF1** and GmWRKY6 synergistically regulate low phosphorus tolerance in soybean.
   - **GmERF1** (ethylene response factor 1), that is mainly expressed in soybean root and localized in the nucleus. Its expression is induced by LP stress and differs substantially in extreme genotypes.
   - **GmERF1** knockout or RNA interference resulted in significant increases in root and P uptake efficiency traits, while the overexpression of **GmERF1** produced an LP-sensitive phenotype and affected the expression of 6 LP stress-related genes.
   - **GmERF1** negatively regulates root development and P absorption.
   - **GmERF1** negatively regulates LP tolerance in soybean.
   - Knockout of **GmERF1** increased ACC content.
