## Functional Descriptions:
   - Mature soybean seeds contain mainly three lipoxygenase isozymes,**GmLOX1**,GmLox2 and GmLox3.
   - Beany flavor induced by three lipoxygenases (LOXs, including **LOX1**, LOX2, and LOX3) restricts human consumption of soybean.
   - GmLox-28 and GmLox-60 were **GmLOX1**gmlox2gmlox3 triple mutants and GmLox-40 was a **GmLOX1**gmlox2 double mutant.
