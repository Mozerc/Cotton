## Functional Descriptions:
   - Further analyses determine that **Dt2** is the corresponding gene and the natural variations in **Dt2** result in significant differential transcriptional levels between the two major haplotypes.
   - Functional characterization reveals that **Dt2** interacts with GmAgl22 and GmSoc1a to physically bind to the promoters of GmAp1a and GmAp1d and to activate their transcription. 
   - The geographic and genetic differentiations of **Dt2** inspired us that modification of **Dt2** may improve the adaptation of soybean.
   - Natural variation in **Dt2** predominantly determines soybean branching.
