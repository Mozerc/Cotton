## Functional Descriptions:
   - GmSTFs directly bind to the promoter regions of isoflavone synthesis-related genes, such as phenylalanine ammoniases GmPAL2.1, **GmPAL2.3** and glycosyltransferase GmUGT2, and promote their transcriptional expression.
   - GmSTF1/2 regulate the expression of approximately 27.9% of the genes involved in soybean isoflavonoid biosynthesis, including GmPAL2.1, **GmPAL2.3**, and GmUGT2.
   - GmSTF1 and GmSTF2 directly associate with DNA cis elements in the promoter regions of GmPAL2.1, **GmPAL2.3**, and GmUGT2 to activate their transcription.
