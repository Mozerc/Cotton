## Functional Descriptions:
   - **GmYSL7** controls iron uptake, allocation, and cellular response of nodules in soybean.
   - Here we demonstrate that **GmYSL7** serves as a key regulator controlling Fe uptake from root to nodule and distribution in soybean nodules.
   - **GmYSL7** is Fe responsive and **GmYSL7** transports iron across the membrane and into the infected cells of nodules.
   - Alterations of **GmYSL7** substantially affect iron distribution between root and nodule, resulting in defective growth of nodules and reduced nitrogenase activity.
   - Our data reveal a new role of **GmYSL7** in controlling Fe transport from host root to nodule and Fe distribution in nodule cells, and uncover a molecular mechanism by which Fe affects nodule number and nitrogenase activity.
