## Functional Descriptions:
   - GmTIR1/**GmAFB3**-based auxin perception regulated by miR393 modulates soybean nodulation.
   - GmTIR1 and **GmAFB3** genes exhibit diverse expression patterns during nodulation and overexpression of GmTIR1 genes significantly increased inflection foci and eventual nodule number.
   - The GmTIR1A, GmTIR1C and **GmAFB3A** genes are the direct targets of soybean miR393.
   - GmTIR1- and **GmAFB3**-mediated auxin signaling, that is spatio-temporally regulated by miR393, plays a crucial role in determinate nodule development in soybean.
