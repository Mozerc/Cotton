## Functional Descriptions:
   - Molecular module **GmPTF1a**/b-GmNPLa regulates rhizobia infection and nodule formation in soybean.
   - **GmPTF1a**/b acts as a key early regulator of nodulation in soybean.
   - Expression pattern of **GmPTF1a**/b during root nodule symbiosis.
   - Manipulating **GmPTF1a**/b significantly impacts rhizobial infection.
   - **GmPTF1a**/b coordinately regulates lateral root and nodule formation.
   - **GmPTF1a** is a direct transcriptional activator of GmNPLa.
