## Functional Descriptions:
   - Overexpressing **GmDnaJA6** and investigate their response to saline–alkaline stress, and identify proteins that interact with **GmDnaJA6**.
   - **GmDnaJA6** responses to saline and alkaline stress.
   - Genome-wide analysis of soybean DnaJA-family genes and functional characterization of **GmDnaJA6** responses to saline and alkaline stress.
   - **GmDnaJA6** was identified as a candidate regulator of saline and alkaline stress resistance and **GmDnaJA6** overexpression lines showed increased soybean saline and alkaline tolerance.
   -  The finding that **GmDnaJA6** expression levels were significantly elevated relative to control at just 3 h post-exposure to saline and alkaline stress suggested that it may be the important mediator of saline-alkaline stress tolerance.
