## Functional Descriptions:
   - The expression of **GmbHLH57** and GmbHLH300, two Fe-related genes expressed in nodules of Gmysl7 mutants.
   - **GmbHLH57** and GmbHLH300 have roles in the nodule response to excessive Fe.
   - **GmbHLH57** to regulate iron uptake in soybean.
