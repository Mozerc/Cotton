## Functional Description:
   - The soybean PLATZ transcription factor **GmPLATZ17** suppresses drought tolerance by interfering with stress-associated gene regulation of GmDREB5.
   - This study sheds light on PLATZ transcription factors and the function of **GmPLATZ17** in regulating drought sensitivity.
   - Overexpression of **GmPLATZ17** increased drought sensitivity in transgenic Arabidopsis.
   - Overexpression of **GmPLATZ17** increased drought sensitivity in soybean hairy roots.
