## Functional Descriptions:
   - MALE STERILITY 3 encodes a plant homeodomain-finger protein for male fertility in soybean
   - **MS3** encodes a transcription factor with a PHD-finger.
   - **MS3** affects the carbohydrate metabolism.
   - Transgenetic analysis indicated that **MS3** knockout resulted in nonfunctional pollen and no self-pollinated pods, and RNA-seq analysis revealed that **MS3** affects the expression of genes associated with carbohydrate metabolism.
   - The fertility of mutant **MS3** can restore under long-d conditions.
