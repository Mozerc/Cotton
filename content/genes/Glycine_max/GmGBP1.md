## Functional Descriptions:
   - Natural variation in **GmGBP1** promoter affects photoperiod control of flowering time and maturity in soybean.
   - The accumulation of the **GmGBP1** transcript is regulated by photoperiod and the circadian clock.
   - Suppression of **GmGBP1** resulted in delay of soybean maturity.
   - The gene interference of **GmGBP1** also caused a reduction in photoperiod response sensitivity (PRS) before flowering in soybean.
   - **GmGBP1** functioned as a positive regulator upstream of GmFT2a and GmFT5a to activate the expression of GmFULc to promote flowering on short days.
