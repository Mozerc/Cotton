## Functional Descriptions:
   - **GmNPLa** was specifically induced by rhizobium inoculation in root hairs. Manipulation of **GmNPLa** produced remarkable effects on IT and nodule formation.
   - Molecular module GmPTF1a/b-**GmNPLa** regulates rhizobia infection and nodule formation in soybean.
   - **GmNPLa** was specifically induced by rhizobium inoculation in root hairs.
   - GmPTF1a/b displayed similar expression patterns as **GmNPLa**, and manipulation of GmPTF1a/b also severely influenced nodulation traits.
   - Altering expression of **GmNPLa** significantly affects both nodulation and lateral root growth.
