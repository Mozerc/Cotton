## Functional Descriptions:
   - **Fg2** Encodes a flavonol 3-O-glucoside (1 -> 6) rhamnosyltransferase.
   - Linkage mapping, molecular cloning and functional analysis of soybean gene **Fg2** encoding flavonol 3-O-glucoside (1 → 6) rhamnosyltransferase.
   - These results indicate that GmF3G6″Rt encodes a flavonol 3-O-glucoside (1 → 6) rhamnosyltransferase and it probably corresponds to the **Fg2** gene. GmF3G6″Rt was designated as UGT79A6 by the UGT Nomenclature Committee.
