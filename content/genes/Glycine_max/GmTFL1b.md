## Functional Descriptions:
   - Association of Stem Growth Habit with DNA Polymorphisms of **GmTFL1b**.
   - Predicted Function of **GmTFL1b** in the Control of Stem Growth Habit and Flowering.
   - The **GmTFL1b** transcript accumulated in the SAMs during early vegetative growth in both the determinate and indeterminate lines but thereafter was abruptly lost in the determinate line.
   - Suggest that Dt1 encodes the **GmTFL1b** protein and that the stem growth habit is determined by the variation of this gene.
