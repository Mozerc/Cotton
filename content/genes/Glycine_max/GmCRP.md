## Functional Descriptions:
   - Genetic variation in **GmCRP** contributes to nodulation in soybean.
   - Overexpression and RNA interference analysis of **GmCRP** in nodulation.
   - **GmCRP** was identified as a candidate gene associated with NopC, and **GmCRP** was found to be induced by NopC to positively regulate nodulation.
   - **GmCRP** may be associated with defense genes in influencing nodulation in soybean.
