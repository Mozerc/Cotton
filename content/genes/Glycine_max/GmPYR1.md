## Functional Descriptions:
   - GmNF-YC14, which formed a heterotrimer with GmNF-YA16 and GmNF-YB2 to activate the **GmPYR1**-mediated abscisic acid (ABA) signalling pathway to regulate stress tolerance in soybean.
   - Overexpression of GmNF-YC14 or **GmPYR1** could increase yield per plant, grain plumpness, and stem base circumference, thus indicating improved adaptation of soybean plants to drought conditions.
   - GmNF-YC14 and **GmPYR1** have potential for application in the improvement of drought tolerance in soybean plants.
   - Field trials showed that overexpression of GmNF-YC14 or **GmPYR1** could increase yield per plant, grain plumpness, and stem base circumference, thus indicating improved adaptation of soybean plants to drought conditions.
