## Functional Descriptions:
   - The B-type response regulator **GmRR11d** mediates systemic inhibition of symbiotic nodulation.
   - **GmRR11d** is induced by rhizobia and low level cytokinin, and **GmRR11d** can suppress the transcriptional activity of GmNSP1 on GmNIN1a to inhibit soybean nodulation.
   - **GmRR11d** positively regulates cytokinin response and its binding on the GmNIN1a promoter is enhanced by cytokinin.
   - **GmRR11d** governs a transcriptional program associated with nodulation attenuation and cytokinin response activation essential for systemic regulation of nodulation.
