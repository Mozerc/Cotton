## Functional Descriptions:
   - Suggests that floral promotion by **GmSWN** via downregulation of GmFLC-like may be conserved in soybean.
   - **GmSWN** photoperiodic flowering time regulation.
   - Latitudinal distribution of **GmSWN** alleles among 187 resequenced ELs with the maturity genotype e1-as/E2. Latitude of origination was used a proxy for relative flowering time.
   - **GmSWN** on chromosome 03 (Glyma.03G224300) was identified as a putative ortholog of the Arabidopsis SWN gene.
