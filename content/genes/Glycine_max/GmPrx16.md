## Functional Descriptions:
   - Natural allelic diversities of **GmPrx16** confer drought tolerance in soybean.
   - **GmPrx16** as a key gene responsible for drought tolerance in soybean natural populations.
   - **GmPrx16** was highly associated with the drought tolerance variation in soybean germplasm, suggesting that **GmPrx16** might be the candidate gene in the association locus on chromosome 16
   - **GmPrx16** was highly expressed in the root and mature leaf tissues . Either dehydration stress treatment or withholding water treatment could induce the transcription of **GmPrx16**.
