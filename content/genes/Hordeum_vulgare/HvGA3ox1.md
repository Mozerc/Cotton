## Functional Descriptions:
   - We revealed that CRISPR/Cas9-induced GA3ox1 mutations increased seed dormancy to an ideal level that could benefit the malting industry.
   - We conclude that selecting **HvGA3ox1** alleles offers a new opportunity for developing barley varieties with optimal stature, longer coleoptile and additional agronomic traits.
   - Genetic variants within **HvGA3ox1** were characterized to define and identify functional haplotypes associated with flowering time, plant height and grain yield components.
   - CRISPR/Cas9-mediated knockout **HvGA3ox1** increased the seed dormancy period but maintained the intrinsic germinative vigour.
