## Functional Descriptions:
   - This study reveals the molecular mechanism of **HVP10** underlying salt tolerance and highlights its potential in improving crop salt tolerance
   - Vacuolar H+-pyrophosphatase **HVP10** enhances salt tolerance via promoting Na+ translocation into root vacuoles.
   - **HVP10** was mainly expressed in roots and upregulated in response to salt stress. After salt treatment for 3 weeks, **HVP10** knockdown (RNA interference) and knockout (CRISPR/Cas9 gene editing) barley plants showed greatly inhibited growth and higher shoot Na+ concentration, Na+ transportation rate and xylem Na+ loading relative to wild-type (WT) plants.
