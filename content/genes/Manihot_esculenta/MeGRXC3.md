## Functional Descriptions:
   - We demonstrated the roles of **MeGRXC3** in regulating activity of catalase at both transcriptional and post-translational levels, therefore involving in ROS homeostasis and stomatal movement in responses of cassava to drought stress.
   - CC-type glutaredoxin, **MeGRXC3**, associates with catalases and negatively regulates drought tolerance in cassava.
   - **MeGRXC3**, was associated with activity of catalase in the leaves of 100 cultivars (or unique unnamed genotypes) of cassava under drought stress.
   - Overall, we demonstrated the roles of **MeGRXC3** in regulating activity of catalase at both transcriptional and post-translational levels, therefore involving in ROS homeostasis and stomatal movement in responses of cassava to drought stress.
