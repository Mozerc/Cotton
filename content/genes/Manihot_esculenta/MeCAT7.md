## Functional Descriptions:
   - Antioxidant gene catalases 7 (**MeCAT7**) expression and antiviral response.
   - The interaction between CP, MeRAV1, and MeRAV2 interfered with the protein phosphorylation of MeRAV1 and MeRAV2 individually at Ser45 and Ser44 by the protein kinase, thereby weakening the transcriptional activation activity of MeRAV1 and MeRAV2 on melatonin biosynthetic genes, MeCAT6 and **MeCAT7** dependent on the protein phosphorylation of MeRAV1 and MeRAV2.
   - It has been previously shown that MeRAV1 and MeRAV2 directly bind to the promoters of tryptophan decarboxylase 2 (MeTDC2), tryptamine 5-hydroxylase (MeT5H), N-aceylserotonin O-methyltransferase 1 (MeASMT1), catalases 6 (MeCAT6), and **MeCAT7** and activate their transcripts.
