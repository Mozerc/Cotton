## Functional Descriptions:
   - **MeMYB2** bound to the MeTT8 promoter and blocked its expression under both normal and chilling conditions, thereby inhibiting anthocyanin accumulation.
   - Down-regulation of **MeMYB2** leads to anthocyanin accumulation and increases chilling tolerance in cassava.
   - We previously generated **MeMYB2**-RNAi transgenic cassava with increased chilling tolerance. Here we report that **MeMYB2**-RNAi transgenic cassava accumulated less ROS but more cyanidin-3-O-glucoside than the wild type under early chilling stress.
   - **MeMYB2** appears to act as an inhibitor of chilling-induced anthocyanin accumulation during the rapid response of cassava to chilling stress.
