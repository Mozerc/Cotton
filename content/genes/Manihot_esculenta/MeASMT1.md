## Functional Descriptions:
   - Melatonin synthesis genes N-aceylserotonin O-methyltransferase 1 (**MeASMT1**) expression and antiviral response.
   - In cassava, MeRAV1, MeRAV2, and MeRAV5 improve cassava resistance against CBB by directly activating the transcripts of melatonin biosynthesis genes (MeTDC2, MeT5H, and **MeASMT1**).
   - It has been previously shown that MeRAV1 and MeRAV2 directly bind to the promoters of tryptophan decarboxylase 2 (MeTDC2), tryptamine 5-hydroxylase (MeT5H), N-aceylserotonin O-methyltransferase 1 (**MeASMT1**), catalases 6 (MeCAT6), and MeCAT7 and activate their transcripts.
