## Functional Descriptions:
   - Targeted knockout of early nodulin-like 3 (**MusaENODL3**) gene in banana reveals its function in resistance to Xanthomonas wilt disease.
   - **MusaENODL3** may play a potential role in Xcm infection and subsequent disease development.
   - This study focused on understanding whether the inactivation of the endogenous **MusaENODL3** gene in banana genome could provide resistance to BXW disease with the goal of developing durable resistance by multiplexing key candidate genes.
