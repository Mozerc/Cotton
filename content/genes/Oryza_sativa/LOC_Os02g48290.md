## Functional Descriptions:
   - Os**NTRB** was highly expressed in roots and shoots of one, two and three week-old rice seedlings, whereas the expression of OsNTRA in shoots was much higher than roots.
   - OsNTRC is chloroplastic type NTR whereas OsNTRA and Os**NTRB** are cytoplasmic/mitochondrial type NTR.
