## Functional Descriptions:
   - Os**CCD1**, a novel small calcium-binding protein with one EF-hand motif, positively regulates osmotic and salt tolerance in rice.
   - Os**CCD1** transcript levels were transiently induced by osmotic stress and salt stress through the calcium-mediated ABA signal.
