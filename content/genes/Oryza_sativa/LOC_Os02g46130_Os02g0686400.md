## Functional Descriptions:
   - The Rice Aspartyl-tRNA Synthetase **YLC3** Regulates Amino Acid Homeostasis and Chloroplast Development Under Low Temperature..
   - Moreover, uncharged tRNA-Asp accumulation and phosphorylation of the translation initiation factor eIF2Î± was detected in the mutant, suggesting that **YLC3** regulates the homeostasis of amino acid metabolism and chloroplast thylakoid development through modulation of processes during protein synthesis.
   - Map-based cloning identified that **YLC3** encodes an aspartyl-tRNA synthetase which is localized in cytosol and mitochondria.
