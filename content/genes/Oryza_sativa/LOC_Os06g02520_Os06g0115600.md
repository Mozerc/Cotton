## Functional Descriptions:
   - **OsIPD3**, an ortholog of the Medicago truncatula DMI3 interacting protein IPD3, is required for mycorrhizal symbiosis in rice..
   - **OsIPD3** mutants are unable to form mycorrhizal symbiosis.
