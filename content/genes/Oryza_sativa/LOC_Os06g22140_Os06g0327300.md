## Functional Descriptions:
   - OsNTRC is chloroplastic type NTR whereas Os**NTRA** and OsNTRB are cytoplasmic/mitochondrial type NTR.
   - OsNTRB was highly expressed in roots and shoots of one, two and three week-old rice seedlings, whereas the expression of Os**NTRA** in shoots was much higher than roots.
