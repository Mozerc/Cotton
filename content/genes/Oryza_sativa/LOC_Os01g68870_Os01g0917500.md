## Functional Descriptions:
   - We propose that the recovered protein interaction together with slow development under low temperature compensates for the defective tapetum initiation, which further restores **OsTMS15** fertility.
   - **OsTMS15** encodes an LRR-RLK protein MULTIPLE SPOROCYTE1 (MSP1) which was reported to interact with its ligand to initiate tapetum development for pollen formation.
