## Functional Descriptions:
   - In this study, we found that a blue light signal triggers reduction of active gibberellin content in rice seedlings with simultaneous repression of two gibberellin 20-oxidase genes (OsGA20ox2 and Os**GA20OX4**) and acute induction of four gibberellin 2-oxidase genes (OsGA2ox4-OsGA2ox7).
   - In parallel, phytochrome B with auxiliary action of phytochrome A mediates repression of GA 20-oxidase genes (OsGA20ox2 and Os**GA20OX4**).
