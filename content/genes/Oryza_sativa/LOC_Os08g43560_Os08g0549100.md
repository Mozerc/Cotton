## Functional Descriptions:
   - The results imply that **OsAPX4** gene has an important role in leaf senescence pathway mediated by ROS signaling.
   - Silencing of **OsAPX4** and OsAPX3 expression in RNAi**OsAPX4** did not affect the growth of plants under growth chamber conditions, but aging transgenic plants interestingly displayed an early senescence phenotype.
