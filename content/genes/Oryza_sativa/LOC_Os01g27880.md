## Functional Descriptions:
   - In situ hybridization showed that **OsSPP**s were strongly expressed in vegetative shoot apex, young panicle, developing panicle, and the early developing florets.
   - **OsSPP**s are located in both the undifferentiated cells, and the early differentiated cells at the shoot apex.
