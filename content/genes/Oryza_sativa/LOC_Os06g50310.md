## Functional Descriptions:
   - Oryzae effector AvrPiz-t interacts with the bZIP-type transcription factor **APIP5** in the cytoplasm and suppresses its transcriptional activity and protein accumulation at the necrotrophic stage.
   - Silencing of **APIP5** in transgenic rice leads to cell death, and the phenotype is enhanced by the expression of AvrPiz-t.
