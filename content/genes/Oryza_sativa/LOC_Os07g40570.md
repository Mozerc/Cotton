## Functional Descriptions:
   - Assays of disease in OsWRKY10KD and **OsWRKY88**KD lines following infection with an incompatible Xoo race, which induced Xa1-mediated resistance in wild-type plants, showed that OsWRKY10 and **OsWRKY88** were positive regulators of Xa1-mediated resistance.
   - In the second transcriptional regulatory cascade, OsWRKY47 acted downstream of OsWRKY10, and **OsWRKY88** acted upstream.
