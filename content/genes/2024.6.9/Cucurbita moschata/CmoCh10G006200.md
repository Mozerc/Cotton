## Functional Descriptions:
   - CmoNAC1 in pumpkin rootstocks improves salt tolerance of grafted cucumbers by binding to the promoters of CmoRBOHD1, CmoNCED6, **CmoAKT1|2**|**CmoAKT1|2** and CmoHKT1|1 to regulate H**CmoAKT1|2**O**CmoAKT1|2**, ABA signaling and K+/Na+ homeostasis.
   - The function of CmoNAC1 was analyzed using root transformation and RNA-seq, and we found that pumpkin CmoNAC1 promoted the production of H**CmoAKT1|2**O**CmoAKT1|2** and ABA via CmoRBOHD1 and CmoNCED6, respectively, and regulated K+/Na+ homeostasis via **CmoAKT1|2**|**CmoAKT1|2**, CmoHKT1|1, and CmoSOS1 to improve salt tolerance of grafted cucumbers.
