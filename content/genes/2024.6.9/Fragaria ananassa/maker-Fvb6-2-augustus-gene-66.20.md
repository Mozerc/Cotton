## Functional Descriptions:
   - Global ubiquitinome analysis reveals the role of E3 ubiquitin ligase **FaBRIZ** in strawberry fruit ripening .
   - Demonstrated that **FaBRIZ**, a RING-type E3 ligase, functions as a negative regulator of ripening in strawberry fruit.
   - **FaBRIZ** is composed of 513 amino acids, containing a RING-type zinc finger domain (RING), a BRCA-1 binding protein domain (BRAP2), and a zinc-finger ubiquitin binding domain (ZF-UBP).
   - These data indicated that **FaBRIZ** has E3 ubiquitin ligase activity in vitro and can catalyse its self-ubiquitination.
   - **FaBRIZ** functions as an E3 ubiquitin ligase to negatively regulate strawberry fruit ripening.
