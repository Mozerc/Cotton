## Functional Descriptions:
   - The transcriptional activity of LjbHLH7 was suppressed by the interaction with the L. japonicus JASMONATE-ZIM DOMAIN protein **LjJAZ4**.
   - A JAZ protein, **LjJAZ4**, was also isolated that can interact with LjbHLH7 and reduce its transcriptional activation capacity.
   - We infer that JA signaling may regulate LjbHLH7 through **LjJAZ4**, thereby regulating the expression of CNglc synthase genes in L. japonicus.
   - We propose that LjbHLH7 acts as an activator and **LjJAZ4** acts as a repressor of JA-induced regulation of CNglc biosynthesis in L. japonicus.
