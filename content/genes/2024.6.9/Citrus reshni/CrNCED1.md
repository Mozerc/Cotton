## Functional Descriptions:
   - Molecular cloning and characterization of **CrNCED1**, a gene encoding 9-cis-epoxycarotenoid dioxygenase in Citrus reshni, with functions in tolerance to multiple abiotic stresses.
   - The steady-state mRNA levels of **CrNCED1** were progressively elevated by dehydration and ABA, only transiently induced by cold, but not affected by salt. 
   - **CrNCED1** was revealed to be stress-responsive, and its ectopic expression in tobacco conferred enhanced tolerance to dehydration, drought, high salinity and oxidative stresses.
   - **CrNCED1** overexpression confers enhanced tolerance to multiple abiotic stresses, which may be, at least in part, ascribed to the positive activation of the stress-responsive genes.
