## Functional Descriptions:
   - Characterization of a Wheat R2R3-MYB Transcription Factor Gene, **TaMYB19**, Involved in Enhanced Abiotic Stresses in Arabidopsis.
   - We also found that the overexpression of **TaMYB19**-B resulted in changes in several physiological indices and altered the expression levels of a number of abiotic stress-related genes, allowing the plants to overcome adverse conditions.
   - The overexpression of **TaMYB19** confers increased tolerance to salt stress in transgenic Arabidopsis.
   - These results indicate that the **TaMYB19** protein plays an important role in plant stress tolerance and that modification of the expression of this protein may improve abiotic stress tolerance in crop plants.
