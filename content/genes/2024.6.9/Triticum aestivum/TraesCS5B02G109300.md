## Functional Descriptions:
   - The Calcium-Dependent Protein Kinase **TaCDPK27** Positively Regulates Salt Tolerance in Wheat.
   - Taken together, the decrease in NaCl tolerance in **TaCDPK27**-silenced seedlings was due to excessive ROS accumulation and subsequent aggravation of the NaCl-induced PCD. **TaCDPK27** may be essential for positively regulating salt tolerance in wheat seedlings.
   - Silencing of the calcium-dependent protein kinase **TaCDPK27** improves wheat resistance to powdery mildew.
   - Overexpressed **TaCDPK27**-mCherry fusions required enhanced autophagy activity in wheat protoplast under carbon starvation.
   - These results suggested that **TaCDPK27** negatively regulates wheat resistance to PW infection, and functionally links with autophagy in wheat.
