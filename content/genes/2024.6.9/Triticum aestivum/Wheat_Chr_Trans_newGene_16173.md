## Functional Descriptions:
   - Candidate powdery mildew resistance gene (**RGA4**) in wheat landrace cultivar Hongyoumai discovered using SLAF and BSR-seq.
   - A **RGA4**-like gene, which has one NB-ARC, five LRR domains, and one LRR_3 was identified and cloned and belonged to the typical NBS-LRR-type gene.
   - One wheat powdery mildew resistance gene, a **RGA4**-like gene, which has one NB-ARC, five LRR domains, and one LRR_3 was identified and cloned and belonged to the typical NBS-LRR-type gene.
   - This suggests that a **RGA4**-like gene from Hongyoumai may be the candidate Pm gene or one part plays an important role in resistance to wheat powdery mildew.
