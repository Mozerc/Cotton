## Functional Descriptions:
   - Silencing of **StLTP10** results in reduced resistance to P. infestans.
   - Characterization of **StLTP10**-overexpressing and knockdown lines indicated that **StLTP10** positively regulates plant resistance to P. infestans.
   - Our findings reveal the molecular mechanism of **StLTP10** in P. infestans defence and suggest that **StLTP10** may act as an integrator of multiple disease-resistant pathways.
   - Taken together, our results provide insight into the role of **StLTP10** in resistance to P. infestans and suggest candidates to enhance broad-spectrum resistance to pathogens in potato.
