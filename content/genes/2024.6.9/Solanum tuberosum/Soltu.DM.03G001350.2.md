## Functional Descriptions:
   - StSN2 interacts with the brassinosteroid signaling suppressor **StBIN2** to maintain tuber dormancy.
   - In addition, the interaction between StSN2 and **StBIN2** was verified by yeast two-hybrid, luciferase complementation experiments and co-immunoprecipitation. Bioinformatics analysis and site-directed mutagenesis confirmed the critical role of cysteine residues of **StBIN2** in its binding to StSN2.
   - Altogether, our results indicate that StSN2 interacts with **StBIN2** through key cysteine residues and **StBIN2** maintains tuber dormancy by affecting ABA and BR signaling.
   - Interaction between StSN2 and **StBIN2** increased the activity of the **StBIN2** enzyme, inhibited the expression of StBZR1, and suppressed BR signaling.
