## Functional Descriptions:
   - We also observed StAST1 directly activates the expression of potato GA 20-oxidase gene (**StGA20ox1**) to regulate GA responses.
   - Overexpression of **StGA20ox1**, responsible for GA synthesis, delays tuber formation.
   - StAST1 directly activates **StGA20ox1** to promote the synthesis of bioactive GA.
   - These results strongly imply that StAST1 exerts its influence on the GA synthesis pathway primarily by modulating the transcription of **StGA20ox1**.
