## Functional Descriptions:
   - **ZmMS1|ZmLBD30**/**ZmMS1|ZmLBD30**-orchestrated transcriptional regulatory networks precisely control pollen exine development.
   - Using an ms1 mutant with aberrantly thickened exine, we cloned a male-sterility gene, **ZmMS1|ZmLBD30**, which encodes a tapetum-specific lateral organ boundary domain transcription factor, **ZmMS1|ZmLBD30**. 
   - **ZmMS1|ZmLBD30**/**ZmMS1|ZmLBD30** is initially turned on by a transcriptional activation cascade of ZmbHLH51-ZmMYB84-ZmMS7, and then it serves as a repressor to shut down this cascade via feedback repression to ensure timely tapetal degeneration and proper level of exine.
   - The **ZmMS1|ZmLBD30**/**ZmMS1|ZmLBD30**-orchestrated transcriptional regulatory networks modulate pollen exine development.
