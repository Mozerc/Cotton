## Functional Descriptions:
   - The apple DNA-binding one zinc-finger protein **MdDof54** promotes drought resistance.
   - Identification of the **MdDof54** gene from apple and document its positive roles in apple drought resistance.
   - By contrast, compared with nontransgenic plants, **MdDof54**-overexpressing plants had higher photosynthesis rates and shoot hydraulic conductivity under long-term drought stress.
   - Taken together, our results provide new information on the functions of **MdDof54** in plant drought stress resistance as well as resources for apple breeding aimed at the improvement of drought resistance.
