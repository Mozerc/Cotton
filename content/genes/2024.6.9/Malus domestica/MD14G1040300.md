## Functional Descriptions:
   - Apple U-box-type E3 ubiquitin ligase **MdPUB23** reduces cold-stress tolerance by degrading the cold-stress regulatory protein MdICE1.
   - **MdPUB23**, positively regulated the cold-stress response in apple, overexpression of **MdPUB23** in transgenic apple enhanced sensitivity to cold stress.
   - **MdPUB23** directly interacted with MdICE1, promoting the ubiquitination-mediated degradation of the MdICE1 protein through the 26S-proteasome pathway and reducing the MdICE1-improved cold-stress tolerance in apple.
   - The PUB23â€“ICE1 ubiquitination module may play a role in maintaining ICE1 protein homeostasis and preventing overreactions from causing damage to plants.
