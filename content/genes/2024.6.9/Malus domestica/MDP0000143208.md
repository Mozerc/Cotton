## Functional Descriptions:
   - Brassinosteroid signaling regulator BIM1 (**MdBIM1**) integrates brassinolide and jasmonic acid signaling during cold tolerance in apple.
   - Investigated the role of **MdBIM1**, a key BR signaling component, in plant cold stress. **MdBIM1** played a positive role in the regulation of cold tolerance through directly activating the expression of MdCBF1 and had an interaction with MdCBF2 to enhance the MdCBF2-activated transcription of COR genes.
   - The **MdBIM1** protein could be degraded by the E3 ubiquitin ligase MdATL73.
   - Our results not only reveal that **MdBIM1** may act as an integrator of BR and JA signals to regulate cold stress but also provide insights into the posttranslational regulatory network of BR signaling in plants.
