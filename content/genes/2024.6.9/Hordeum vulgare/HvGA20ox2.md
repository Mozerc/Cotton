## Functional Descriptions:
   - The **HvGA20ox2|sdw1**.a allele from Jotun resulted from a total deletion of the **HvGA20ox2|sdw1** gene.
   - The results provided further proof that the gibberellin 20-oxidase gene (**HvGA20ox2|sdw1**) is the functional gene of the barley **HvGA20ox2|sdw1** mutants.
   - Partial or total loss of function of the **HvGA20ox2|sdw1** gene could be compensated by enhanced expression of its homolog HvGA20ox1 and HvGA20ox3.
   - The 7-bp deletion in **HvGA20ox2|sdw1** gene reduced plant height by approximately 13 cm and delayed flowering time by 3â€“5 days in the barley segregating DHLs population independently on environmental cue.
   - The results provide further support for GA20 oxidase 2 (**HvGA20ox2|sdw1**) as the functional gene for the **HvGA20ox2|sdw1** locus. We also observed that 52 barley varieties/lines displayed the short stature without the **HvGA20ox2|sdw1**.a, **HvGA20ox2|sdw1**.c and **HvGA20ox2|sdw1**.d alleles in this population.
