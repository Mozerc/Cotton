## Functional Descriptions:
   - Deletion either of the two internal repeats in **PlAvh142** abolished the cell death-inducing activity.
   - Knockout of **PlAvh142** resulted in significantly attenuated P. litchii virulence on litchi plants, whereas the **PlAvh142**-overexpressed mutants were more aggressive.
   - These data indicated that **PlAvh142** could be recognized in plant cytoplasm and is an important virulence RXLR effector of P. litchii.
   - These results indicate that **PlAvh142** contribute to the P. litchii virulence during infection in its native host litchi.
