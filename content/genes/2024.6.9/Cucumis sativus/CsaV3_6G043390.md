## Functional Descriptions:
   - Suggesting that **CsTOE3** may be involved in the regulatory network of cucumber fruit spine partly depending on ethylene pathway.
   - **CsTOE3** is highly expressed in cucumber fruit spines and peels.
   - **CsTOE3** knockout results in altered flower phenotype and inhibition of fruit spine initiation.
   - **CsTOE3** may regulate cucumber spines initiation partly through the ethylene pathway.
