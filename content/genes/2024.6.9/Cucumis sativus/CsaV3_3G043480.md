## Functional Descriptions:
   - CsMLO8 and CsCRK2 competitively interact with the C-terminus of **CsRbohD** to affect CsCRK2-**CsRbohD** module-mediated ROS production during PM defense.
   - Results revealed that CsMLO8/CsML11 interact with CsCRK2 and **CsRbohD** to regulate PM resistance in cucumber stem.
   - CsMLO8/CsML11 are required for full susceptibility of cucumber stem to powdery mildew and interact with CsCRK2 and **CsRbohD**.
