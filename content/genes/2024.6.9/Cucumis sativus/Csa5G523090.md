## Functional Descriptions:
   - A CsTu-TS1 regulatory module promotes fruit tubercule formation in cucumber.
   - Our research also suggests that **CsTS1** controls the development of cucumber fruit tubercules through increasing auxin content.
   - **CsTS1** positively increases cell size and tubercule formation.
   - **CsTS1** promoter is an important factor affecting gene transcription, and it influences the development of cucumber tubercules.
   - **CsTS1** increases auxin signalling and levels in fruit tubercules.
