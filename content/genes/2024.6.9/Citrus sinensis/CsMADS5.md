## Functional Descriptions:
   - A fruit ripening-associated transcription factor **CsMADS5** positively regulates carotenoid biosynthesis in citrus.
   - Transient overexpression of **CsMADS5** in citrus induced fruit coloration and enhanced carotenoid concentrations.
   - Similar results were observed from stable overexpression of **CsMADS5** in tomato fruits and citrus calli, even though the effect of **CsMADS5** on carotenoid metabolism in transgenic citrus calli was limited.
   - **CsMADS5** physically interacted with a positive regulator CsMADS6, indicating that **CsMADS5** may form an enhancer complex with CsMADS6 to synergistically promote carotenoid accumulation.
