## Functional Descriptions:
   - GhMYB30-**GhMUR3** affects fiber elongation and secondary wall thickening in cotto.
   - **GhMUR3** encodes a xyloglucan galactosyltransferase that is essential for xyloglucan synthesis and is highly expressed during fiber elongation.
   - Overall, these results revealed that **GhMUR3** under the regulation of GhMYB30 and plays an essential role in cotton fiber elongation and secondary wall thickening.
