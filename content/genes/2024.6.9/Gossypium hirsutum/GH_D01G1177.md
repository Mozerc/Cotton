## Functional Descriptions:
   - **GhCKX14** responding to drought stress by modulating antioxi-dative enzyme activity in Gossypium hirsutum compared to CKX family genes.
   - Functionally identified via virus-induced gene silencing (VIGS) found that the **GhCKX14** gene improved drought resistance by modulating the antioxidant-related activitie.
   - Silencing of **GhCKX14** compromises cotton resistance to drought stress.
   - These results suggest that silencing of the **GhCKX14** gene could reduce the physiological performance tolerance of cotton plants to drought stress.
