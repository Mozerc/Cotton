## Functional Descriptions:
   - We characterized the gene expression, subcellular localization, and in vivo functions of a Nicotiana benthamiana small GTPase belonging to the RabE family, designated **NbRabE1**.
   - The **NbRabE1** promoter drove strong Î²-glucuronidase reporter expression in young tissues containing actively dividing cells and in stomata guard cells.
   - Virus-induced gene silencing of **NbRabE1** resulted in pleiotropic phenotypes, including growth arrest, premature senescence, and abnormal leaf development.
   - Ectopic expression of the dominant-negative mutant of **NbRabE1** in Arabidopsis thaliana resulted in retardation of shoot and root growth accompanied by defective root hair formation.
