## Functional Descriptions:
   - **VvWRKY5** enhances white rot resistance in grape by promoting the jasmonic acid pathway.
   - Transient injection and stable overexpression (in grape calli and Arabidopsis) demonstrated that **VvWRKY5** positively regulated grape resistance to white rot.
   - **VvWRKY5** was overexpressed in grape, JA content was also increased.
   - **VvWRKY5** played a key role in regulating JA biosynthesis and signal transduction as well as enhancing white rot resistance in grape. Our results also provide theoretical guidance for the development of elite grape cultivars with enhanced pathogen resistance.
