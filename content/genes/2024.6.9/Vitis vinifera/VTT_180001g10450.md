## Functional Descriptions:
   - The Basic Leucine Zipper Transcription Factor ABSCISIC ACID RESPONSE ELEMENT-BINDING FACTOR2 (**VvABF2**) Is an Important Transcriptional Regulator of Abscisic Acid-Dependent Grape Berry Ripening Processes.
   - **VvABF2** transcripts mainly accumulated in the berry, from the onset of ripening to the harvesting stage, and were up-regulated by ABA.
   - **VvABF2** overexpression strongly increased the accumulation of stilbenes that play a role in plant defense and human health (resveratrol and piceid).
   - In addition, the firmness of fruits from tomato (Solanum lycopersicum) plants overexpressing **VvABF2** was strongly reduced. These data indicate that **VvABF2** is an important transcriptional regulator of ABA-dependent grape berry ripening.
