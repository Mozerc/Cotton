## Functional Descriptions:
   - Genome-wide identification of BAM genes in grapevine (Vitis vinifera L.) and ectopic expression of **VvBAM1** modulating soluble sugar levels to improve low-temperature tolerance in tomato.
   - **VvBAM1** was highly expressed in the leaves, petioles, stems, and roots of overexpressing tomato lines. 
   - Effects of ectopic **VvBAM1** expression on antioxidant enzyme activities in tomato plants under LT stress.
   - **VvBAM1** overexpression promotes ROS scavenging and improves cold tolerance ability by modulating starch hydrolysis to affect soluble sugar levels in tomato plants.
