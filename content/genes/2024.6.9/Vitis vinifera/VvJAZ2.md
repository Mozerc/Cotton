## Functional Descriptions:
   - Determined that VvWRKY5 regulated the JA response by directly binding to the promoters of **VvJAZ2** (a JA signaling suppressor) and VvMYC2 (a JA signaling activator), thereby inhibiting and activating the transcription of **VvJAZ2** and VvMYC2, respectively.
   - VvWRKY5 participated in jasmonic acid-mediated disease resistance by binding to the **VvJAZ2** and VvMYC2 promoters.
   - **VvJAZ2** enhances the transcriptional regulatory activity of VvWRKY5 on its target genes **VvJAZ2** and VvMYC2.
