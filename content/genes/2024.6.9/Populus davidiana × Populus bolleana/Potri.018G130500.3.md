## Functional Descriptions:
   - Overexpression of the **PtSOS2** gene improves tolerance to salt stress in transgenic poplar plants.
   - Compared to the wild-type control, transgenic plants constitutively expressing **PtSOS2**TD exhibited more vigorous growth and produced greater biomass in the presence of high concentrations of NaCl.
   - Poplar calcineurin B-like proteins PtCBL10A and PtCBL10B regulate shoot salt tolerance through interaction with **PtSOS2** in the vacuolar membrane.
   - Taken together, our results suggest that **PtSOS2** could serve as an ideal target gene to genetically engineer salt-tolerant trees.
