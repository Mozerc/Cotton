## Functional Descriptions:
   - miR156a-targeted SBP-Box transcription factor **SlSPL13** regulates inflorescence morphogenesis by directly activating SFT in tomato.
   - SPL13 positively regulates SFT expression by directly binding regulatory cis-elements in the SFT promoter.
   - SPL13 is a key factor in miR156a-regulated tomato plant development.
