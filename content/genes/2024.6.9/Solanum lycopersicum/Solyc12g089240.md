## Functional Descriptions:
   - Report that the tomato B-BOX (BBX) transcription factors SlBBX20 and **SlBBX21** interact with SlHY5 and bind to the SlHY5 promoter to activate its transcription.
   - UV-B-induced SlHY5 expression and SlHY5-controlled UV-B responses are normal in slbbx20 and **SlBBX21** single mutants, but strongly compromised in the slbbx20 **SlBBX21** double mutant.
