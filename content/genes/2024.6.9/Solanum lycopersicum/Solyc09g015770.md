## Functional Descriptions:
   - Four group III WRKY proteins (SlWRKY52, SlWRKY59, SlWRKY80, and **SlWRKY81**) interacted with SlWRKY30, and **SlWRKY81** silencing increased tomato susceptibility to RSI.
   - Both SlWRKY30 and **SlWRKY81** activated SlPRSTH2a/b/c/d expression by directly binding to their promoters.
   - SlWRKY30 and **SlWRKY81** synergistically regulate resistance to RSI by activating SlPR-STH2a/b/c/d expression in tomato.
