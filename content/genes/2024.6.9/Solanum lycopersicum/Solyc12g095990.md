## Functional Descriptions:
   - SlRBP1 promotes translational efficiency via **SleIF4A2** to maintain chloroplast function in tomato.
   - Silencing of SlRBP1 significantly reduced the association of **SleIF4A2** with its RNA targets, and led to less occupancy of polysomes on RNA targets
   - SlRBP1 interacts with the eukaryotic translation initiation factor (**SleIF4A2**) and promotes its association with RNA targets.
