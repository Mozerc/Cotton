## Functional Descriptions:
   - Mutation of **SlARC6** leads to tissue-specific defects in chloroplast development in tomato.
   - Knockdown and knockout of **SlARC6** in wild-type tomato inhibit chloroplast division, indicating the conserved function of **SlARC6**.
   - In addition, prolonged lighting can partially rescue the albinic phenotypes in su mutants, implying that light may promote SlACR6-independent chloroplast development.
   - Our results verify the role of SlACR6 in chloroplast division in tomato and uncover the tissue-specific regulation of chloroplast development.
