   - Among the candidate proteins, we identified a homeodomain-leucine zipper (HD-Zip) class II protein and named it **CaHAT1** (Capsicum annuum homeobox ABA signalling related- transcription factor 1). 
   - Following the application of drought stress, **CaHAT1**-silenced pepper plants exhibited drought-sensitive phenotypes with reduced ABA-mediated stomatal closure and lower expression of stress-responsive genes compared with control plants. 
   - Pepper homeobox abscisic acid signalling-related transcription factor 1, **CaHAT1**, plays a positive role in drought response.
