## Functional Descriptions:
   - **PoWRKY71** is involved in Paeonia ostii resistance to drought stress by directly regulating light-harvesting chlorophyll a/b-binding 151 gene.
   - **PoWRKY71** positively responded to drought stress with significantly enhanced expression patterns and overexpressing **PoWRKY71** in tobacco greatly improved plant tolerance to drought stress, whereas silencing **PoWRKY71** in P. ostii resulted in a drought-intolerant phenotype.
   - The above results indicated that **PoWRKY71** activated the expression of PoCAB151, thus stabilising photosynthesis via regulating chloroplast homeostasis and chlorophyll content in P. ostii under drought stress.
   - **PoWRKY71** played a positive role in resisting drought stress, and it directly activated PoCAB151 transcription, which participated in stabilising photosynthesis via regulating chloroplast homeostasis and chlorophyll content.
