## Functional Descriptions:
   - A homologous system indicated that drought treatment increased **PoCAB151** promoter activity. Interactive assays revealed that PoWRKY71 directly bound on the W-box element of **PoCAB151** promoter and activated its transcription.
   - **PoCAB151** overexpressing plants demonstrated increased drought tolerance, together with significantly higher chlorophyll contents and photosynthesis, whereas these indices were dramatically lower in **PoCAB151**-silenced P. ostii.
   - The above results indicated that PoWRKY71 activated the expression of **PoCAB151**, thus stabilising photosynthesis via regulating chloroplast homeostasis and chlorophyll content in P. ostii under drought stress.
