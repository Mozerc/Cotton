## Functional Descriptions:
   - Overexpression of **PeJAZ2** in poplar notably increased water deficit tolerance and WUE in both severe and mild drought stress by regulating ABA signaling rather than ABA synthesis.
   - A **PeJAZ2** overexpression line was shown to have greater ABA-induced stomatal closure and hydrogen peroxide (H2O2) production. Collectively, this evidence establishes a mechanism in which **PeJAZ2** acts as a positive regulator in response to drought stress via ABA-induced stomatal closure caused by H2O2 production.
   - **PeJAZ2** and PeOST1 interact as a putative model for the involvement of MeJA and ABA cross-signaling in drought stress-mediated stomatal movement.
