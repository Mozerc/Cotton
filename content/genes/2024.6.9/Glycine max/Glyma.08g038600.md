## Functional Descriptions:
   - **GmYUC2a** mediates auxin biosynthesis during root development and nodulation in soybean.
   - In this study, we analyzed the YUCCA (YUC) gene family of soybean and identified **GmYUC2a** as an important regulator of auxin biosynthesis that modulates nodulation.
   - Our findings demonstrate that **GmYUC2a**-mediated local auxin biosynthesis contributes to rhizobial infection and nodule organogenesis, including nodule senescence, in soybean nodules.
   - Our results suggest that **GmYUC2a** plays an important role in regulating both root growth and nodulation by modulating auxin balance in soybean.
