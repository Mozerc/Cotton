## Functional Descriptions:
   - To investigate the role of BraA.REF6 and **BraA.ELF6** on vegetative development, we compared the mutant lines to wild-type B. rapa plants and observed that the overall plant architecture of all braA.ref6 and **BraA.ELF6** mutant lines were not severely affected.
   - We considered both braA.ref6-1 and braA.ref6-2 strong alleles regarding flowering time. In the case of **BraA.ELF6** alleles, **BraA.ELF6**-1 showed a pronounced acceleration of flowering time whereas **BraA.ELF6**-2 manifested a narrow phenotype suggesting that it is a weak allele. 
   - **BraA.ELF6** represses flowering by modulating FLC expression in B. rapa.
   - These data are consistent with BraA.REF6 and **BraA.ELF6** modulating H3K27me3 levels genome-wide and participating in the regulation of several biological processes in B. rapa.
