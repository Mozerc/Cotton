## Functional Descriptions:
   - Identified **BpGRP1** (glycine-rich RNA-binding protein), a Betula platyphylla gene that is induced under salt stress.
   - The physiological and molecular responses to salt tolerance were investigated in both **BpGRP1**-overexpressing and suppressed conditions.
   - Based on the current data, we established a direct molecular link among **BpGRP1**, BpGRF3 and BpmiR396c for improving the salt tolerance of birch.
   - **BpGRP1** acts downstream of BpmiR396c/BpGRF3 to confer salt tolerance in Betula platyphylla.
