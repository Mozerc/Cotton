## Functional Descriptions:
   - **BpGRF3** (growth-regulating factor 3) was identified as a regulatory factor upstream of BpGRP1.
   - Demonstrated that overexpression of **BpGRF3** significantly increased the salt tolerance of birch, whereas the grf3-1 mutant exhibited the opposite effect.
   - Based on the current data, we established a direct molecular link among BpGRP1, **BpGRF3** and BpmiR396c for improving the salt tolerance of birch.
   - The overexpression of **BpGRF3** in birch improves tolerance to salt stress.
