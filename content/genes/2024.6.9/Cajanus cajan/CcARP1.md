## Functional Descriptions:
   - Melatonin-mediated **CcARP1** alters F-actin dynamics by phosphorylation of CcADF9 to balance root growth and salt tolerance in pigeon pea.
   - The **CcARP1** overexpression lines (**CcARP1**-OE) displayed an increased transgenic root length and resistance to salt stress, whereas **CcARP1** RNA interference lines (**CcARP1**-RNAi) presented the opposite phenotype. 
   - Findings reveal that melatonin-induced **CcARP1** maintains F-actin dynamics balance by phosphorylating CcADF9, thereby promoting root growth and enhancing salt tolerance.
   - Salt resistance of pigeon pea is enhanced by overexpressing **CcARP1** in transgenic hairy roots, but weakened by RNAi suppression.
