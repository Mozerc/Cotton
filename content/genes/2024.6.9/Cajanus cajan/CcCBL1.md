## Functional Descriptions:
   - The pigeon pea CcCIPK14-**CcCBL1** pair positively modulates drought tolerance by enhancing flavonoid biosynthesis.
   - **CcCBL1**-overexpressed plants displayed higher survival rates upon drought stress as well as higher expression of flavonoid biosynthetic genes and flavonoid content.
   - CcCIPK14-overexpressed plants in which **CcCBL1** transcript levels were reduced by RNA interference had lower survival rates, which indicated **CcCBL1** in the same pathway as CcCIPK14.
   - Our results demonstrate a role for the CcCIPK14-**CcCBL1** complex in drought stress tolerance through the regulation of flavonoid biosynthesis in pigeon pea.
