## Functional Descriptions:
   - ClAGO1 and **ClAGO5** expression levels were highly induced by CGMMV infection. The results of ClAGO1 and **ClAGO5** overexpression and silencing experiments suggest that these genes play central roles in watermelon's antiviral defence.  
   - Furthermore, co-immunoprecipitation and bimolecular fluorescence complementation experiments showed that ClAGO1 interacts with **ClAGO5** in vivo, suggesting that ClAGO1 and **ClAGO5** co-regulate watermelon defence against CGMMV infection.
   - We also identified the ethylene response factor (ERF) binding site in the promoters of the ClAGO1 and **ClAGO5** genes, and ethylene (ETH) treatment significantly increased **ClAGO5** expression.
