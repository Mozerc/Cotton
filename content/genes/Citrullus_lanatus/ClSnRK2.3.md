## Functional Descriptions:
   - **ClSnRK2.3** negatively regulated watermelon fruit ripening by manipulating the biosynthesis of sucrose, ABA and GA4.
   - We previously revealed that the abscisic acid (ABA) signaling pathway gene **ClSnRK2.3** might influence watermelon fruit ripening.
   - **ClSnRK2.3** negatively regulated watermelon fruit ripening by manipulating the biosynthesis of sucrose, ABA and GA4. Altogether, these findings revealed a novel regulatory mechanism in non-climacteric fruit development and ripening.
