## Functional Descriptions:
   - Our findings highlight the crucial role of **MsFLS13** in alfalfa in response to saline-alkali stress and provide a novel genetic resource for creating saline-alkali-resistant alfalfa through genetic engineering.
   - Flavonol synthase gene **MsFLS13** regulates saline-alkali stress tolerance in alfalfa.
   - Overexpression of **MsFLS13** in alfalfa promoted plant tolerance to saline-alkali stress by enhancing flavonol accumulation, antioxidant capacity, osmotic balance, and photosynthetic efficiency.
   - Our research provides novel insights into the role of **MsFLS13** in regulating antioxidant capacity in alfalfa under saline-alkali stress, and a new gene resource for cultivating saline-alkali-tolerant alfalfa.
