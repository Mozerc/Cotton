## Functional Descriptions:
   - Protein interaction between AhCaM4 and **AhSAMS1**.
   - AhCaM4 positively modulated the function of binding protein **AhSAMS1** under salt stress.
   - WT plants may coordinate with **AhSAMS1** to regulate plant salt tolerance of plants.
   - **AhSAMS1**-induced methylation is regulated by CaM, providing a novel strategy for increasing the tolerance of plants to salt stress.
