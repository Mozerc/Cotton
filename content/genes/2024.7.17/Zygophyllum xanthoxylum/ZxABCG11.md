## Functional Descriptions:
   - Overexpression of **ZxABCG11** alters the phenotype and cuticle ultrastructure of leaves in alfalfa.
   - Overexpression of **ZxABCG11** enhances the tolerance of transgenic alfalfa to drought and heat.
   - Transgenic alfalfa overexpressing **ZxABCG11** showed higher water use efficiency (WUE) and stronger photosynthesis capacity under drought or heat conditions.
   - Overexpression of **ZxABCG11** increased cuticular wax deposition, reducing cuticle permeability.
   - Transgenic alfalfa overexpressing **ZxABCG11** showed higher biomass yields under field conditions of hot, arid regions.
   - Overexpression of **ZxABCG11** resulted in upregulation of wax biosynthesis and transport related genes.
   - Overexpression of **ZxABCG11** confers enhanced drought and heat tolerance in alfalfa by increasing accumulation of cuticular wax.
   - **ZxABCG11** overexpression affects the expression of wax biosynthesis and transport related genes.
