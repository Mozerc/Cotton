## Functional Descriptions:
   - The maize late embryogenesis abundant protein **ZmDHN13** positively regulates copper tolerance in transgenic yeast and tobacco.
   - Transcription of **ZmDHN13** could be induced by drought, low temperature, H2O2, CuCl2, and treatment with ABA
   - One molecule of **ZmDHN13** could bind up to 14 Cu
   - Overexpression of **ZmDHN13** increased yeast GS115 tolerance to copper stress.
   - Overexpression of **ZmDHN13** increase transgenic tobacco tolerance to copper stress.
