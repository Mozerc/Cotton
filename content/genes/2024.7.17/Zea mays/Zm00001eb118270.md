## Functional Descriptions:
   - Expression of **ZmDRR206** in maize seedlings responded rapidly to Fusarium graminearum infection and light illumination.
   - **ZmDRR206** functions in maize kernel development and seedling growth.
   - **ZmDRR206** positively regulates maize disease resistance to F. graminearum-induced stalk rot.
   - **ZmDRR206** affects both primary and secondary metabolism in maize seedlings.
   - Defense-related transcriptional reprogramming was induced by **ZmDRR206** overexpression.
   - **ZmDRR206** functions in CWI maintenance by regulating cell-wall biosynthesis.
   - **ZmDRR206** interacts with a cellulose synthase subunit ZmCesA10.
