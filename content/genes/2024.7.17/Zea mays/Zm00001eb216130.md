## Functional Descriptions:
   - 12 genes in eight types, including ZmbHLH51, ZmbHLH122, ZmTGA9-1/-2/-3, ZmTGA10, ZmMYB84, ZmMYB33-1/-2, **ZmPHD11** and ZmLBD10/27, were identified as maize new GMS genes by using DNA sequencing, phenotypic and cytological analyses.
   - **ZmPHD11** is the maize ortholog of the reported GMS genes OsTIP3 and AtMMD1 encoding PHD finger TFs.
   - **ZmPHD11** is required for maize male fertility via controlling the formation of anther cuticle and Ubisch bodies.
   - The results showed that null mutations of eight-type TF genes, including ZmbHLH51, ZmbHLH122, ZmTGA9-1/-2/-3, ZmTGA10, ZmMYB84, ZmMYB33-1/-2, **ZmPHD11** and ZmLBD10/27, resulted in male sterility with different kinds of anther and pollen defects.
