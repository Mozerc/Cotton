## Functional Descriptions:
   - Overexpression of **ZmAL14|ZmAL9** exhibits susceptibility to drought while mutation of **ZmAL14|ZmAL9** enhances drought resistance.
   - An abscisic acid (ABA)-activated protein kinase ZmSnRK2.2 interacts and phosphorylates **ZmAL14|ZmAL9** at T38 residue.
   - Phosphorylation of **ZmAL14|ZmAL9** by ZmSnRK2.2 prevents its binding to the ZmROP8 promoter, thereby releasing the repression of ZmROP8 transcription.
   - Overexpression (OE) of **ZmAL14|ZmAL9** increases drought sensitivity while mutations of **ZmAL14|ZmAL9** enhance drought tolerance in maize.
   - **ZmAL14|ZmAL9** is a negative regulator of drought resistance, which can be phosphorylated by ZmSnRK2.2 through the ABA signaling pathway, thus preventing its suppression on ZmROP8 transcription during drought stress response.
