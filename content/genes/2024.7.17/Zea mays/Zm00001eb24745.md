## Functional Descriptions:
   - **ZmCER1** is positively correlated with plant drought tolerance.
   - **ZmCER1**, encoding a very-long-chain aldehyde decarbonylase, is responsible for the rgd4 phenotype.
   - Mutations of **ZmCER1** were responsible for the phenotype of rgd4.
   - Overexpressing **ZmCER1** does not affect leaf development.
   - **ZmCER1** is an ER-localized protein.
   - **ZmCER1** might be associated with biotic and abiotic stress responses.
