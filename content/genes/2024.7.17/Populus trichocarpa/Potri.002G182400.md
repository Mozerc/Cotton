## Functional Descriptions:
   - The miR6445-NAC029 module regulates drought tolerance by regulating the expression of glutathione S-transferase U23 and reactive oxygen species scavenging in Populus.
   - Both Crispr-NAC029 and PtrGSTU23 overexpressing plants showed higher levels of PtrGSTU23 transcript and GST activity while accumulating less reactive oxygen species (ROS).
   - **PtrNAC029** was further verified to directly bind to the promoters of glutathione S-transferase U23 (GSTU23) and inhibit its expression.
   - Our research reveals the crucial role of the miR6445-NAC029-GSTU23 module in enhancing poplar drought tolerance by regulating ROS homeostasis.
