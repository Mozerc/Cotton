## Functional Descriptions:
   - The different GUS expression levels driven by the truncated promoters suggest that the Box4-1 and Box4-2 are responsible for higher transcriptional activity of **GsWRKY40** promoter than GmWRKY40 promoter.
   - Overexpression of **GsWRKY40** improves salt tolerance in transgenic Arabidopsis.
   - Overexpression of **GsWRKY40**-GsbHLH92 improved soybean salt stress tolerance.
   - **GsWRKY40** and GsbHLH92 synergistically regulate GmSPOD1 expression.
