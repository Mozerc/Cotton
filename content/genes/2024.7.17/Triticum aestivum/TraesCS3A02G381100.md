## Functional Descriptions:
   - **TaSnRK2.4** is sensitive to drought and ABA signaling and contributes to drought response by regulating osmotic stress and physiological defense processes.
   - Transgene analysis revealed that **TaSnRK2.4** and TaABF2 positively regulate drought tolerance whereas TaPP2C01 acts negatively by modulating stomatal movement, osmotic adjustment, reactive oxygen species (ROS) homeostasis, and root morphology.
   - **TaSnRK2.4** is sensitive to drought and ABA signaling and contributes to drought response by regulating osmotic stress and physiological defense processes.
   - Expression analysis of **TaSnRK2.4** in plants subjected to drought and ABA signaling.
   - Cis-elements DRE and ABRE, and the binding sites for MYB and MYC TF proteins, act synergistically in regulating transcription of **TaSnRK2.4** in responding to drought and ABA signaling.
   - **TaSnRK2.4** signaling module contributes to plant response to drought stress.
