## Functional Descriptions:
   - Loss of function of CRT1a (**BnCTR1a**) reduces plant susceptibility to Verticillium longisporum in both Arabidopsis thaliana and oilseed rape.
   - BnaA09g15400D is the ‘active’ CRT1a copy in Brassica napus being involved in the plant–fungus interaction.
   - The loss of function of CRT1a results in activation of the ethylene signalling pathway, which may contribute to reduced susceptibility.
