## Functional Description:
   - **BnaA3.MYB28** was responsible for high leaf/low seed GSL content and could explain 30.62% of the total leaf GSL variation in the low seed GSL panel and was not fixed during double-low rapeseed breeding.
   - A 1356-bp insertion in **BnaA3.MYB28** resulted in lower GSL content in leaves.
   - BnaA03g40190D (**BnaA3.MYB28**) gene was not fixed and was responsible for high leaf/low seed GSL content.
