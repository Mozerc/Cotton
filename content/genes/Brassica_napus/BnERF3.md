## Functional Descriptions:
   - **BnERF3** overexpression increases the resistance to salt stress.
   - **BnERF3** was significantly induced in response to salt stress in the WT, and compared with the WT plants, the OE-**BnERF3** lines under salt stress presented significantly higher expression levels.
   - These results revealed 234 candidate genes associated with salt stress response, and two novel candidate genes, BnCKX5 and **BnERF3**, were experimentally verified to regulate the salt stress tolerance of B. napus.
