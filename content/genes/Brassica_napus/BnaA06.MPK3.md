## Functional Descriptions:
   - **BnaMPK3s** promote organ size by interacting with BnaARF2s in Brassica napus.
   - These two **BnaMPK3s** were highly expressed in vegetative tissues and increasingly expressed in seeds during development.
   - Suggesting MPK3s play conserved functions in promoting organ size.
   - In this study, several lines of evidence showed that the **BnaMPK3s**-BnaARF2s module is critical for the regulation of organ size and yield in B.
