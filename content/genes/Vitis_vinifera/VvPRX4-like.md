## Functional Descriptions:
   - Among the 657 candidate genes, we identified three PRX-type lignin biosynthesis-related genes:VvPRXN1,**VvPRX4-like**,and VvPRX47.
   - Compared with the expression in WT plants, the expression of **VvPRX4-like** and VvPRX47 was upregulated in transgenic lines under drought stress; however, the expression of VvPRX N1 was downregulated in transgenic lines compared with the WT under drought stress.
   - Three lignin biosynthetic genes (VvPRX N1, **VvPRX4-like**, and VvPRX47) as candidate target genes of VlbZIP30 and found that the expression of VvPRX N1 and VvPRX47 was induced by drought stress in WT plants, and the expression of **VvPRX4-like** and VvPRX47 was induced by drought stress in the transgenic plants.
