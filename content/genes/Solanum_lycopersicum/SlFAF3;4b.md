## Functional Descriptions:
   - The other members of the SlFAF gene family, SlFAF1/2b, **SlFAF3**/4a and **SlFAF3**/4b, are negative regulators of tomato flowering and faf1/2b, faf3/4a and faf3/4b single mutants all display early flowering.
   - We generated a series of early flowering mutants between SlFAF1/2b, **SlFAF3**/4a and FAF3/4b using the CRISPR/Ca9 editing system.
   - Different members of the SlFAF gene family have contrasting effects on tomato flowering.
