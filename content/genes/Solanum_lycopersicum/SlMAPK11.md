## Functional Descriptions:
   - We found a gene in tomato that encodes a mitogen-activated protein kinase (**SlMAPK11**) and contributes to seed germination, as identified by its different expression levels in seeds showing either low or high optimum germination temperatures.
   - MAPK11 regulates seed germination and ABA signaling in tomato by phosphorylating SnRKs.
   - Our results demonstrate that ABA signaling in tomato is affected by a mechanism that depends on MAPK11 phosphorylating SnRKs, and this ultimately influences seed germination.
