## Functional Descriptions:
   - In particular, two genes that encode GA biosynthetic enzymes SlGA3ox1 and **SlGA3ox2** have decreased expression in STTM159 as compared with wild type.
   - These results indicate Sly-miR159 may activate GA biosynthesis in these critical stages of ovary/fruit development by promoting the expression of SlGA3ox1 and **SlGA3ox2**.
   - Together, these results corroborate the gene expression analysis and suggest that SlGAMYB2 only targets **SlGA3ox2** in controlling GA biosynthesis.
   - we demonstrate that Sly-miR159 promotes GA biosynthesis largely through the direct repression of the GA biosynthetic gene **SlGA3ox2** by SlGAMYB2.
