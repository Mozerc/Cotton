## Functional Description:
   - Knock-out of Brassinosteroid-insensitive2 (**SlBIN2**), a negative regulator of BR signaling upstream of SlBZR1, promoted fruit ripening and carotenoid accumulation.
   - SlBZR1 overexpression or **SlBIN2** knock-out promoted fruit ripening with elevated ethylene and carotenoid levels.
   - **SlBIN2** interacts with SlBZR1 to negatively regulate tomato fruit ripening.
