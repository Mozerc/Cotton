## Functional Descriptions:
   - Overexpression (OE) or knock-down of **SlMYB31**, an MYB transcription factor that physically interacts with Wo in vivo and in vitro, produces marked changes in wax composition, and whereas Wo knock-down inhibits wax accumulation in **SlMYB31**-OE lines, **SlMYB31** knock-down inhibits wax accumulation in Wo-OE lines, implying that these two genes function in the same pathway.
   - Wo and **SlMYB31** cooperatively control tomato cuticular wax biosynthesis by regulating the expression of SlCER6.
   - Wo and **SlMYB31** potentially cooperate in regulating wax biosynthesis by mediating SlCER6 expression in tomato.
   - These results demonstrate that Wo and **SlMYB31** cooperatively control tomato cuticular wax biosynthesis by regulating the expression of SlCER6.
