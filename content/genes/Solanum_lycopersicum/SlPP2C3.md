## Functional Descriptions:
   - The role of the tomato ABA co-receptor type 2C phosphatase **SlPP2C3**, a negative regulator of ABA signaling and fruit ripening.
   - Expression of **SlPP2C3** was ABA-inducible, which was negatively correlated with fruit ripening.
   - Tomato plants with suppressed **SlPP2C3** expression exhibited enhanced sensitivity to ABA, while plants overexpressing **SlPP2C3** were less sensitive to ABA.
   - Our findings demonstrate that **SlPP2C3** plays an important role in the regulation of fruit ripening and fruit glossiness in tomato.
