## Functional Description:
   - The JA signaling inhibitor Sl**JAZ2** represses the activity of H and HL via physical interaction, resulting in the activation of THM1, a negative regulator of trichome formation.
   - Sl**JAZ2** can suppress Woolly, a key transcription factor regulating trichome formation in tomato.
