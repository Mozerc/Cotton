## Functional Descriptions:
   - Tomato receptor-like cytosolic kinase RIPK confers broad-spectrum disease resistance without yield penalties.
   - Overexpression of **SlRIPK** confers broad-spectrum disease resistance without a yield penalty in tomato plants.
   - The **SlRIPK** mutants and **SlRIPK**-overexpressing transgenic plants did not exhibit significant growth retardation or yield loss. These results suggest that overexpression of **SlRIPK** confers broad-spectrum disease resistance without a yield penalty in tomato plants.
