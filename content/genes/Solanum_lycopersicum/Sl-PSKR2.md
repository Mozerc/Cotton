## Functional Descriptions:
   - The receptors of PSK in tomato, PSKR1 and PSKR2, belong to the LRR motif rich transmembrane receptor protein kinase (LRR-RLK).
   - Phytosulfokine (PSK) regulates the defense mechanism of tomato response to Botrytis cinerea.
   - The gene silencing approaches used in this study confirmed that only Sl-PSKR1 was the major transducer of the PSK signal in the regulation of B. cinerea defenses, as silencing of **Sl-PSKR2** did not lead to any significant effects on plant responses to B. cinerea.
