## Functional Descriptions:
   - Anthocyanin Fruit encodes an R2R3-MYB transcription factor, **SlAN2-like**, activating the transcription of SlMYBATV to fine-tune anthocyanin content in tomato fruit.
   - The R2R3-MYB transcription factor **SlAN2-like** (Solyc10g086290) was considered the best candidate gene for Aft.
   - **SlAN2-like** is the best candidate gene for the Aft locus.
   - The expression of **SlAN2-like** is responsive to light, and the functional **SlAN2-like** can activate the expression of both anthocyanin biosynthetic genes and their regulatory genes, suggesting that **SlAN2-like** acts as a master regulator in the activation of anthocyanin biosynthesis.
   - Expression of a functional **SlAN2-like** gene driven by the fruit-specific promoter in a tomato cultivar led to the activation of the entire anthocyanin biosynthesis pathway and high-level accumulation of anthocyanins in both the peel and flesh.
