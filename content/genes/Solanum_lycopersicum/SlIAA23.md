## Functional Descriptions:
   - **SlIAA23** could interact with SlARF6 in vivo and in vitro, and promoted the AMS and phosphorus uptake.
   - SlARF6 and **SlIAA23** played a contrary role in strigolactone (SL) synthesis and accumulation in AMF-colonized roots of tomato plants.
   - SIAA23 promotes AMS and phosphorus uptake in tomato roots.
