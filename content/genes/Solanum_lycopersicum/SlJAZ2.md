## Functional Descriptions:
   - The JA signaling inhibitor **SlJAZ2** represses the activity of H and HL via physical interaction, resulting in the activation of THM1, a negative regulator of trichome formation.
   - **SlJAZ2** can suppress Woolly, a key transcription factor regulating trichome formation in tomato.
   - Among these JAZs, JAZ2 was previously reported to inhibit trichome formation when overexpressed in tomato.
