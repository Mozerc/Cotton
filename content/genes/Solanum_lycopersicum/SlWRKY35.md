## Functional Descriptions:
   - **SlWRKY35** positively regulates carotenoid biosynthesis by activating the MEP pathway in tomato fruit.
   - The outcome of our study defines **SlWRKY35** as a positive regulator of carotenoid biosynthesis in tomato.
   - **SlWRKY35** can directly activate the expression of the 1-deoxy-d-xylulose 5-phosphate synthase (SlDXS1) gene to reprogramme metabolism towards the 2-C-methyl-d-erythritol 4-phosphate (MEP) pathway, leading to enhanced carotenoid accumulation.
   - We also show that the master regulator SlRIN directly regulates the expression of **SlWRKY35** during tomato fruit ripening.
