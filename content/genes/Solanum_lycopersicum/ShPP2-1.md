## Functional Descriptions:
   - Interactions between **ShPP2-1**, an F-box family gene, and ACR11A regulate cold tolerance of tomato.
   - Overexpression of **ShPP2-1** in cultivated tomato (AC) reduced cold tolerance by intensifying damage to cell membranes.
   - Overexpression of **ShPP2-1** reduced the cold tolerance of tomato.
