## Functional Descriptions:
   - WHIRLY1 maintains leaf photosynthetic capacity in tomato by regulating the expression of RbcS1 under chilling stress.
   - WHY1 protein from Solanum lycopersicum (**SlWHY1**) enhanced tomato chilling resistance by regulating the D1 protein and starch content in chloroplasts.
   - **SlWHY1** enhances the Rubisco carboxylation rate under chilling stress.
   - We report that tomato WHIRLY1 (**SlWHY1**) positively regulates the Rubisco level under chilling stress by directly binding to the promoter region of SlRbcS1, resulting in the activation of SlRbcS1 expression.
