## Functional Descriptions:
   - **SlJAZ4**, a negative regulator preferentially expressed in trichomes, as the critical component in JA signaling in tomato trichomes.
   - **SlJAZ4** is the predominant inhibitor of jasmonic acid signaling in trichomes.
   - our findings define SlHD8-**SlJAZ4** as a key module mediating JA-induced trichome elongation in tomato.
