## Functional Descriptions:
   - **Woolly**, interacting with MYB transcription factor MYB31, regulates cuticular wax biosynthesis by modulating CER6 expression in tomato.
   - **Woolly** (Wo), a multicellular trichome regulator, is involved in modulating wax biosynthesis in tomato.
   - As well as expression levels of **Woolly** and Hair, two regulators of trichome initiation and development in tomato.
   - These results demonstrate that **Woolly** and SlMYB31 cooperatively control tomato cuticular wax biosynthesis by regulating the expression of SlCER6.
