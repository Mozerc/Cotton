## Functional Descriptions:
   - The pivotal ripening gene **SlDML2** participates in regulating disease resistance in tomato.
   - Mutation of **SlDML2**, the DNA demethylase gene essential for fruit ripening, affects multiple developmental processes of tomato besides fruit ripening, including seed germination, leaf length and width and flower branching.
   - Loss of **SlDML2** function decreased the resistance of tomato fruits against the necrotrophic fungal pathogen Botrytis cinerea.
   - These results revealed the functional diversity of **SlDML2** in modulating multiple developmental processes in tomato.
