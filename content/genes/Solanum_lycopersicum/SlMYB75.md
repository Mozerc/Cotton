## Functional Descriptions:
   - **SlMYB75**, an MYB-type transcription factor, promotes anthocyanin accumulation and enhances volatile aroma production in tomato fruits.
   - The identification of **SlMYB75** as a key regulator of fruit quality attributes through the transcriptional regulation of downstream genes involved in several metabolic pathways opens new avenues towards engineering fruits with a higher sensory and nutritional quality.
   - Overexpression of a single **SlMYB75** induces anthocyanin accumulation.
