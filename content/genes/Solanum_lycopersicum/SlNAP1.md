## Functional Descriptions:
   - Our findings reveal that **SlNAP1** is a positive regulator of the tomato defense response against multiple stresses and thus may be a potential breeding target for improving crop yield and stress resistance.
   - Transcriptomic and genetic approaches reveal an essential role of the NAC transcription factor **SlNAP1** in the growth and defense response of tomato.
   - **SlNAP1** overexpression dramatically improved drought tolerance in tomato.
   - Analysis of different hormone contents revealed a reduced level of physiologically active gibberellins (GAs) and an increased level of salicylic acid (SA) and abscisic acid (ABA) in the **SlNAP1**-overexpressing plants.
