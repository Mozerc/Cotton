## Functional Descriptions:
   - The Three Tomato GID1s (GID1a, GID1b1, and **GID1b2**) Are Functional GA Receptors.
   - Three receptors, SlGID1a (GID1a), GID1b1, and **GID1b2**, that may guide specific GA responses.
   - gid1 mutants were grown in the field under ambient changing environments, they showed phenotypic instability, the high redundancy was lost, and gid1a exhibited dwarfism that was strongly exacerbated by the loss of another GID1b receptor gene.
