## Functional Descriptions:
   - The role and interaction between transcription factor **NAC-NOR** and DNA demethylase SlDML2 in the biosynthesis of tomato fruit flavor volatiles.
   - **NAC-NOR** target gene lipoxygenase (SlLOXC) that is involved in fatty acid-derived volatile synthesis.
   - **NAC-NOR** activates SlDML2 expression by directly binding to its promoter both in vitro and in vivo.
   - These results reveal a relationship between SlDML2-mediated DNA demethylation and **NAC-NOR** during tomato fruit ripening.
